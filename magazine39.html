【區塊一】英文文章原文

原文 Original Article

The 2022 FIFA World Cup final between Argentina and France drew an audience of nearly 1.5 billion, with the upcoming 2026 edition expected to surpass that number. Compare this with the 2025 Super Bowl, which had about 190 million viewers worldwide.

Winning the World Cup is considered the ultimate honor of a player’s career. Pelé in 1958, Maradona in 1986, and Messi in 2022 are just a few of the players who have achieved historic fame and recognition through the competition.

The World Cup returns to North America for the first time since the United States hosted it in 1994, when it set records for the highest total attendance and average attendance per match. This time around, the competition has already made history. It is the first edition to feature 48 teams, the first to be cohosted by three countries, and the first to include more than 100 matches. The Tunisia vs. Japan fixture, scheduled for June 20 in Monterrey, Mexico, will be the 1,000th match in World Cup history. The tournament also coincides with the United States’ 250th-anniversary celebrations.

There is plenty of drama surrounding the event, both on and off the field. Italy, a four-time champion, has failed to qualify for a third straight time, making it the only former winner not to be part of the 2026 World Cup. Iran’s participation has been called into question amid tensions with the U.S., while diplomatic disputes, ticket pricing, and security concerns add to the controversies surrounding the tournament.

On the field, legendary players such as Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are playing in what is likely to be their last World Cup; exciting stars such as Kylian Mbappé and Lamine Yamal are set to become headliners; and the expanded roster increases the likelihood of dark horses and upsets.

Whether you're a passionate football fan or are simply curious about the world's biggest sporting event, The Pitch will give you the historical perspective and cultural context you need to truly understand what's at stake this summer!

精讀與分析 8 Key Sentence Analyses
1
The 2022 FIFA World Cup final between Argentina and France drew an audience of nearly 1.5 billion, with the upcoming 2026 edition expected to surpass that number.
2022年阿根廷與法國之間的世界盃決賽吸引了近 15 億觀眾，而即將到來的 2026 年屆次預計將超過這一數字。
文法解析：本句使用了「with + 受詞 + 受詞補語」的結構。with the upcoming 2026 edition expected to surpass ... 用來補充說明前句的情況，其中 expected 是過去分詞，表示「被預期」。
draw an audience 吸引觀眾 surpass 超過 upcoming 即將到來的
2
Winning the World Cup is considered the ultimate honor of a player’s career.
贏得世界盃被視為一名球員職業生涯的終極榮譽。
文法解析：Winning the World Cup 是動名詞片語當主詞，視為單數。is considered 為被動語態，後接名詞 the ultimate honor 作為主詞補語。
ultimate 終極的 career 職業生涯 consider A B 將A視為B
3
The World Cup returns to North America for the first time since the United States hosted it in 1994, when it set records for the highest total attendance and average attendance per match.
這是自美國 1994 年舉辦世界盃以來，世界盃首次重返北美，當時創下了最高總入場人數和每場平均入場人數的紀錄。
文法解析：when it set records ... 是非限定形容詞子句（關係副詞用法），補充說明 1994 年發生的事情。
set records 創下紀錄 attendance 入場人數 for the first time 首次
4
It is the first edition to feature 48 teams, the first to be cohosted by three countries, and the first to include more than 100 matches.
這是首屆擁有 48 支球隊參加、首屆由三個國家共同舉辦、以及首屆包含超過 100 場比賽的屆次。
文法解析：本句使用了平行結構 the first to + V。不定詞 to feature, to be cohosted, to include 皆作為形容詞用法，修飾前面的代名詞 first (edition)。
feature 以...為特色 cohost 共同舉辦 edition 版本/屆次
5
Italy, a four-time champion, has failed to qualify for a third straight time, making it the only former winner not to be part of the 2026 World Cup.
四屆冠軍義大利已連續第三次未能晉級，使其成為唯一未能參加 2026 年世界盃的前冠軍隊。
文法解析：逗號後的 making it ... 是分詞構句，表示前面整件事帶來的「附帶結果」。not to be part of 為不定詞的否定，修飾 winner。
fail to 未能... qualify 取得資格/晉級 straight 連續的
6
Iran’s participation has been called into question amid tensions with the U.S., while diplomatic disputes, ticket pricing, and security concerns add to the controversies surrounding the tournament.
由於與美國的緊張關係，伊朗的參與受到了質疑，而外交爭端、門票定價和安全疑慮也增加了圍繞該賽事的爭議。
文法解析：while 在此表示「對比」或「同時存在的情況」。call ... into question 是一個常見片語，表示「對...產生質疑」。
amid 在...之中 dispute 爭端 controversy 爭議
7
On the field, legendary players such as Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are playing in what is likely to be their last World Cup.

legendary 傳奇的 be likely to 很可能 what is ... ...的事物

【區塊四】文法解析

文法重點整理 Grammar Focus

【文法名稱】形容詞子句：where / when / why（關係副詞）

【引句 Quote】
The World Cup returns to North America ... in 1994, when it set records for the highest total attendance...
【句型】 Time/Place + when/where + S + V ...
【核心說明】 關係副詞 when、where、why 用來引導形容詞子句，分別修飾表示時間、地點或原因的先行詞。它們在子句中充當副詞的功能，可替換為「介系詞 + which」。
【使用規則】
  • when：修飾時間（如 year, day, time）。
  • where：修飾地點（如 country, city, place）。
  • why：修飾原因（如 reason）。
  • 非限定用法（加逗號）：僅用於補充說明，拿掉後主句依然完整。
【主題例句】 I remember the year when France won their second World Cup title.（我記得法國贏得第二個世界盃冠軍的那一年。）
【學習提醒】 注意 when/where 後面接完整子句，而 which/that 後面可能接不完整子句（欠缺主詞或受詞）。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】 ✗ This is the stadium which the final match was held.
✓ This is the stadium where the final match was held.
說明：held 是及物動詞的被動，子句完整，修飾地點應用 where 或 in which。

選擇題：I'll never forget the day ________ we first watched a World Cup match together.
(A) which (B) when (C) where (D) who
答案：B。

【文法名稱】名詞子句：whether / if

【引句 Quote】
Whether you're a passionate football fan or are simply curious...
【句型】 Whether ... or ... + S + V
【核心說明】 whether 引導的名詞子句表示「是否」，用於表達不確定或二選一的情況。在本課引句中，它作為副詞用法，表示「無論是...還是...」。
【使用規則】
  • whether ... or not：常用來強調「無論是否」。
  • 與 if 的差異：if 不能放在句首當主詞，也不能直接放在介系詞後面，而 whether 可以。
  • whether to V：whether 後可接不定詞片語，if 則不行。
【主題例句】 Whether Messi plays in the next World Cup is still a secret.（梅西是否會參加下一屆世界盃仍然是個秘密。）
【學習提醒】 當 whether 子句放在句首時，通常翻譯為「無論...」，此時具有副詞子句的功能。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】 ✗ If he comes or not doesn't matter.
✓ Whether he comes or not doesn't matter.
說明：if 引導的名詞子句不可放在句首當主詞。

改錯題：I'm not sure if or not they will win the game. (修正 if 為 whether)

【文法名稱】分詞構句：表附帶結果

【引句 Quote】
Italy ... has failed to qualify ..., making it the only former winner not to be part of the 2026 World Cup.
【句型】 S + V ..., V-ing ...
【核心說明】 當副詞子句的主詞與主要子句主詞相同時，可以化簡為分詞構句。逗號後的 V-ing 常用來表示「附帶發生的結果」或「同時發生的動作」。
【使用規則】
  • 主動關係用 V-ing。
  • 被動關係用 p.p.（通常省略 being）。
  • 表示結果時，通常放在主要子句之後。
【主題例句】 The team won the championship, surprising fans all over the world.（球隊贏得了冠軍，令全世界的球迷感到驚訝。）
【學習提醒】 使用分詞構句時，要確保主要子句的主詞就是該分詞動作的執行者，否則會產生「懸空分詞」的錯誤。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】 ✗ The rain was heavy, caused many delays.
✓ The rain was heavy, causing many delays.
說明：下雨主動導致延誤，應使用 V-ing。

合併句子：The expansion increased the number of teams. It made the competition more exciting. (使用分詞構句)
答案：The expansion increased the number of teams, making the competition more exciting.

練文筆食材 (Writing Expressions)▼
① ... draw an audience of ...（吸引了...觀眾）
→ 適用：描述活動規模或受歡迎程度。
例：The concert drew an audience of thousands.
② ... be expected to surpass ...（預計將超過...）
→ 適用：進行預測或比較時。
例：The new sales figures are expected to surpass last year's records.
③ ... be considered the ultimate honor of ...（被視為...的終極榮譽）
→ 適用：強調某事物的重要性或地位。
例：Winning a Nobel Prize is considered the ultimate honor of a scientist's career.
④ ... set records for ...（創下了...的紀錄）
→ 適用：描述突破性的成就。
例：The movie set records for the highest opening weekend earnings.
⑤ ... be called into question ...（受到了質疑）
→ 適用：討論爭議或不確定的情況。
例：The accuracy of the report has been called into question by experts.
