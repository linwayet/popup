Unit 2｜閱讀填充練習

主題：傳統街區如何轉型

Reading Passage: When an Old District Learns to Face a New Direction

For decades, Harbor Lane was treated as a quiet stop between the train station and the coast. Its wooden houses, narrow alleys, and stone staircases attracted only a few visitors. Recently, however, the district has become more prominent. Young shop owners have founded small businesses there, while older artisans continue to make baskets, wooden beads, and hand-carved signs.

The change did not simply replace tradition with fashion. Instead, local leaders tried to create a careful blend of memory and innovation. A tiny boutique sells clothes made by indigenous designers. A scent studio offers customized fragrances. A restaurant serves gourmet pizzas with local pineapples and mountain herbs. The goal is not to make the neighborhood a tourist mecca, but to let residents earn a living without losing their cultural roots.

Even the map at the visitor center teaches a lesson. Unlike most modern maps, it is not fixed with north at the top. Its orientation changes according to the path a visitor chooses. Some historians explain that map layouts have always reflected power: during the age of European imperialism, many maps became Eurocentric, while today GPS systems are often oriented toward the user. In that sense, the north-up habit may be only a historical blip.

Harbor Lane still faces problems. During a recent storm, a shop window was shattered and several signboards were overturned. Some residents expressed grave concerns about safety. Yet the shop owners did not resort to blaming one another. They repaired the damage, raised money to compensate affected stores, and agreed that community needs should take precedence over quick profits.

學測提醒

針對本學生錯題
1. 搭配先行
看到 concern 要想到 grave / serious；看到 take ___ over 要想到 precedence。
2. 詞形檢查
be perfectly ___ with foreign elements 需要過去分詞 blended，不是原形 blend。
3. 推論詞義
blip 不用硬背，從 less a rule and more a blip 推出「短暫狀態」。
4. 細節回文
閱讀題問 true / inferred 時，回到原文找時間、方向、主詞，不靠印象答。

建議練習順序

第一輪：單字卡只看英文，遮住中文回想意思。第二輪：做 Unit 2 填充，逼自己判斷詞性。第三輪：做 Unit 3 選擇題，練干擾選項。最後把錯題整理成「搭配短語」，例如 dread the thought of V-ing、resort to V-ing、take precedence over。
