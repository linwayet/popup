中譯英分析

題 1 原答案

Now more and more high school English teachers have increased the rate of use English in class.

  • 優點：主詞和現在完成式方向正確，能抓到「越來越多老師已經增加」的核心。
  • 問題：rate of use English 不合語法；use 若放在 of 後面要變 using，更自然是 the percentage/amount of English they use in class。senior high school 比 high school 更貼近學測用語。
建議答案：More and more senior high school English teachers have increased the percentage of English they use in class.
題 2 原答案

They group students by English capable and carry out various listening and speaking activities.

  • 優點：group students 和 carry out various listening and speaking activities 很好，第二個動詞搭配自然。
  • 問題：capable 是形容詞，這裡需要名詞 ability 或 proficiency；「依照」可寫 according to / based on，比 by 更精準。
建議答案：They group students according to their English ability and carry out various listening and speaking activities.

翻譯程度判斷：她能建立英文句子主幹，動詞片語也有一定掌握；扣分關鍵會在詞性、名詞片語和介系詞精準度。作文未作答會讓非選整體風險偏高。
