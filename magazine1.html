區塊一　英文文章原文

Battle of the Balls: Football or Soccer?

As the 2026 FIFA World Cup approaches, an interesting debate kicks off once again: Should the sport be called soccer or football? Both words appear in media and conversations, yet their usage changes across countries…

區塊二　8句精讀

①時間副詞子句開場

As the 2026 FIFA World Cup approaches, an interesting debate kicks off once again: Should the sport be called soccer or football?
隨著 2026 年 FIFA 世界盃接近，一場有趣的辯論再次展開：這項運動應該被稱為 soccer 還是 football？
文法解析：這句以 As + S + V 引導時間副詞子句，表示「隨著……」。主要子句是 an interesting debate kicks off，冒號後面補充辯論的核心問題。Should the sport be called... 使用被動語態，因為重點在「這項運動被如何稱呼」，不是誰命名。
approach：接近debate：辯論kick off：開始

②對等連接與轉折

Both words appear in media and conversations, yet their usage changes across countries.
這兩個字都出現在媒體和日常對話中，然而它們的用法會因國家而異。
文法解析：Both A and B 表示「A 和 B 兩者都」，用來並列兩個名詞。yet 在此作連接副詞，語氣接近 however，表示前後句意轉折。across countries 表示「跨國、在不同國家之間」，不是單一地點。
media：媒體usage：用法across countries：在各國之間

③間接問句

If they refer to the same sport, how did this linguistic split begin, and which one's actually correct?
如果它們指的是同一項運動，這個語言上的分歧是如何開始的？哪一個其實才正確？
文法解析：If they refer to... 是條件副詞子句，提供討論前提。後面兩個疑問句以 how 與 which 開頭，分別詢問起源與正確性。linguistic split 是名詞片語，linguistic 修飾 split，表示「語言上的分裂」。
refer to：指的是linguistic：語言的split：分歧

④非限定補充說明

Its roots can be traced back to 19th-century Britain, where several forms of football were played under different rules.
它的根源可以追溯到 19 世紀的英國，當時那裡有好幾種足球類運動依照不同規則進行。
文法解析：can be traced back to 是情態助動詞加被動語態，表示「可以被追溯到」。逗號後的 where 引導非限定形容詞子句，補充說明 Britain 這個地點。were played 也是被動語態，強調運動被進行，而不是誰在玩。
trace back to：追溯到19th-century Britain：19 世紀英國under different rules：依照不同規則

⑤不定詞表目的

To distinguish association football from rugby football, Oxford University students shortened the word association to a sock and added the playful suffix '-er', common in student slang at the time.
為了區分英式足球和橄欖球，牛津大學學生把 association 這個字縮短成 sock，並加上當時學生俚語中常見、帶有玩笑意味的字尾 -er。
文法解析：句首 To distinguish... 是不定詞片語表目的；這裡的 to 是不定詞，不是介係詞。主要子句有兩個對等動詞：shortened 和 added。句尾 common in student slang at the time 是形容詞片語，補充說明 suffix '-er' 的性質。
distinguish A from B：區分 A 與 Bsuffix：字尾slang：俚語

⑥as 引導時間變化

Over time, as rugby became less central to British sports culture and association football became dominant, the word football came to mean association football alone.
隨著時間過去，當橄欖球在英國運動文化中變得較不核心，而英式足球變得主流時，football 這個字逐漸只指英式足球。
文法解析：as rugby became... 是時間副詞子句，描述背景變化。子句中使用兩個並列的 became，形成文化地位的對比。主要子句的 came to mean 表示「逐漸變成表示……」，常用來描述意義或狀態的漸變。
central to：對……很核心dominant：主導的come to mean：逐漸意指

⑦不定詞與關係子句

Meanwhile, countries such as the US, Canada, and Australia kept the word soccer to avoid ambiguity with other sports that were also called football.
同時，美國、加拿大、澳洲等國保留 soccer 這個字，以避免和其他也被稱為 football 的運動產生歧義。
文法解析：such as 用來舉例，列出屬於 countries 的例子。to avoid ambiguity 是不定詞表目的，說明保留 soccer 的原因。that were also called football 是形容詞子句，修飾 other sports。
such as：例如avoid ambiguity：避免歧義be called：被稱為

⑧對比焦點 rather than

Today, debates over football versus soccer often reflect identity rather than linguistic correctness.
如今，football 與 soccer 之間的爭論往往反映的是身分認同，而不是語言上的正確性。
文法解析：主詞是 debates over football versus soccer，over 表示「關於」。動詞 reflect 表示「反映」，後面接抽象名詞 identity。rather than 用來建立對比，強調真正重點是身分認同，不是語言正確性。
reflect：反映identity：身分認同rather than：而不是

區塊四　文法解析

【名詞子句】Wh- Noun Clauses 疑問詞名詞子句

【引句 Quote】

If they refer to the same sport, how did this linguistic split begin, and which one's actually correct?

【句型】

疑問詞 + S + V

【核心說明】

疑問詞 how、which、why、what 等可以引導問句，也可以引導名詞子句。若放在動詞後當受詞，語序要改成直述句語序，例如 I wonder how this split began，不能寫成 how did this split begin。

【使用規則】

  • 直接問句要倒裝：How did it begin?
  • 間接問句不倒裝：I know how it began.
  • 疑問詞名詞子句可當主詞、受詞或補語。
  • which 常在有限選項中做選擇。

【主題例句】

Many fans still wonder why the same sport has two English names.（許多球迷仍想知道為什麼同一項運動有兩個英文名稱。）

【學習提醒】

中文常直接說「我不知道它怎麼開始」，英文要注意間接問句語序。只要疑問詞子句不是獨立問句，就不要把助動詞放到主詞前面。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ I don't know how did this linguistic split begin.

✓ I don't know how this linguistic split began.（間接問句要用直述語序。）

Choose the correct sentence: A. Can you explain why is soccer used? B. Can you explain why soccer is used? C. Can you explain why does soccer use? D. Can you explain why used soccer?

答案：B。說明：why 引導名詞子句時，後面要接主詞 soccer 加動詞 is used。

【不定詞】Infinitive of Purpose 不定詞表目的

【引句 Quote】

To distinguish association football from rugby football, Oxford University students shortened the word association to a sock and added the playful suffix '-er', common in student slang at the time.

【句型】

To + 原形動詞, S + V ...

【核心說明】

句首的 To distinguish... 表示目的，意思是「為了區分……」。這裡的 to 是不定詞標記，不是介係詞，所以後面必須接原形動詞 distinguish。

【使用規則】

  • 不定詞表目的可放句首，後面通常加逗號。
  • to 後面接原形動詞：to distinguish、to avoid、to explain。
  • 可改寫成 in order to，語氣較正式。
  • 否定目的用 in order not to 或 so as not to。

【主題例句】

To avoid ambiguity, some countries continue to use the word soccer.（為了避免歧義，有些國家繼續使用 soccer 這個字。）

【學習提醒】

看到 to 不要立刻判斷它是介係詞。若後面接原形動詞，它就是不定詞；若後面接名詞或 V-ing，才可能是介係詞。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ To distinguishing the two words, students checked the history.

✓ To distinguish the two words, students checked the history.（不定詞 to 後面接原形動詞。）

Fill in the blank: _____ ambiguity, writers should define key terms clearly. A. Avoid B. To avoid C. Avoiding to D. Avoided

答案：B。說明：句首表示目的，應使用 To avoid。

【介係詞】Despite + N/V-ing 儘管……

【引句 Quote】

By the 1980s, soccer had come to be viewed negatively in the UK as an American expression, despite its British origins.

【句型】

despite + 名詞 / V-ing

【核心說明】

despite 是介係詞，表示「儘管」。它後面接名詞、名詞片語或動名詞，不能直接接完整子句；本文的 despite its British origins 就是 despite 加名詞片語。

【使用規則】

  • despite + N：despite the problem。
  • despite + V-ing：despite facing criticism。
  • 若要接完整子句，改用 although、though 或 even though。
  • despite the fact that + 子句 是較正式的例外結構。

【主題例句】

Despite its British origin, the word soccer is often thought of as American.（儘管 soccer 源自英國，它常被視為美式用語。）

【學習提醒】

despite 和 although 中文都可翻成「儘管」，但文法結構不同。despite 後面不能直接放主詞加動詞，這是高中英文常見錯誤。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Despite soccer started in Britain, many people think it is American.

✓ Although soccer started in Britain, many people think it is American.（despite 不能直接接子句，改用 although。）

Choose the correct sentence: A. Despite it was British, the word changed. B. Despite its British roots, the word changed. C. Despite the word was British, it changed. D. Despite because it was British, it changed.

答案：B。說明：despite 後面接名詞片語 its British roots，結構正確。

折疊四　練文筆食材⌄

① As + S + V, S + V（隨著……，……）
→ 適用：描寫背景變化或趨勢。
例：As technology improves, online learning becomes more flexible.

② Both A and B（A 和 B 兩者都）
→ 適用：並列兩個重點。
例：Both teachers and students need clear communication.

③ If + S + V, wh- question（如果……，那麼……？）
→ 適用：提出議題或引導討論。
例：If students learn online, how can schools keep them motivated?

④ be traced back to + 時間/地點（可追溯到……）
→ 適用：介紹歷史起源。
例：This tradition can be traced back to the early twentieth century.

⑤ To + V, S + V（為了……，……）
→ 適用：說明目的。
例：To reduce stress, many students exercise after school.

⑥ Through + N, S + V（透過……，……）
→ 適用：說明方法或過程。
例：Through daily practice, learners can build stronger writing skills.

⑦ Over time, S + V（隨著時間過去，……）
→ 適用：描述逐漸發生的變化。
例：Over time, small habits can lead to major improvement.

⑧ as A became..., B became...（當 A 變得……，B 也變得……）
→ 適用：比較兩個同步變化。
例：As cities became larger, public transportation became more important.

⑨ such as + N（例如……）
→ 適用：舉例說明。
例：Many countries, such as Japan and Korea, value public manners.

⑩ to avoid + N/V-ing（為了避免……）
→ 適用：解釋採取行動的原因。
例：To avoid confusion, the teacher wrote the rules on the board.

⑪ had come to be viewed as...（逐漸被視為……）
→ 適用：描述社會觀感的形成。
例：Online learning had come to be viewed as a useful option.

⑫ despite + N/V-ing（儘管……）
→ 適用：表達讓步與對比。
例：Despite the pressure, the team finished the project on time.

⑬ rather than + N/V-ing（而不是……）
→ 適用：強調真正重點。
例：Good learning depends on steady effort rather than luck.

⑭ The next time + S + V, remember that...（下次……時，記得……）
→ 適用：結論或提醒讀者。
例：The next time you feel nervous, remember that preparation builds confidence.

⑮ long before + S + V（早在……之前）
→ 適用：強調時間先後。
例：People shared stories long before books became common.
