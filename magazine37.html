2026年度代表色：雲舞白

【區塊一】英文文章原文 Original Article

Every year, the Pantone Color Institute announces a Color of the Year — not simply to set a fashion trend, but to reflect the mood and needs of people around the world. For 2026, Pantone selected PANTONE 11-4201 Cloud Dancer, a soft, billowy white described as "a whisper of calm and peace in a noisy world". This marks the first time in Pantone's history that a white tone has been chosen as the defining color of the year.

The choice of Cloud Dancer speaks directly to the emotional state of modern society. Leatrice Eiseman, executive director of the Pantone Color Institute, explained that "the cacophony that surrounds us has become overwhelming," making it harder for people to focus or connect with their inner thoughts. In an age of constant digital stimulation, Cloud Dancer acts as a visual antidote — a quiet, open space that encourages reflection, and simplification. Pantone vice president Laurie Pressman added that people are simply "looking for respite" and want to "step back" from the noise of daily life.

Beyond fashion and design, Cloud Dancer carries a deeper symbolic meaning. Like a blank canvas, it represents the human desire for a fresh start and new possibilities. It reminds us that clarity does not come from adding more to our lives, but from removing what is unnecessary. At a time when the world feels fast, loud, and uncertain, a softer white offers something surprisingly powerful: the promise of stillness, renewal, and hope.

【區塊四】文法解析 Grammar

【文法名稱】Relative Clause 形容詞子句：who / which / that

【引句 Quote】
"...a quiet, open space that encourages rest, reflection, and simplification."
【句型】N + that/which/who + V ...
【核心說明】形容詞子句用來修飾前面的名詞（先行詞），提供更多資訊或限制其範圍。本句中的 that encourages... 修飾 space，說明該空間的功能。
【使用規則】
  • who：指人。
  • which：指物。
  • that：指人或物，主要用於限定用法。
  • that 不用於非限定用法（逗號之後）。
【主題例句】The designer chose a color that represents hope.（設計師選擇了一種代表希望的顏色。）
【學習提醒】看到 which 前面有逗號時，它可能修飾前面的整個句子。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ The color, that is very soft, is white.

✓ The color, which is very soft, is white.（非限定子句不可用 that）

試題：The students ______ finish the task will get a prize. (A) who (B) which (C) whose (D) whom

答案：(A)

【文法名稱】Noun Clause 名詞子句：that 子句作受詞

【引句 Quote】
"Leatrice Eiseman explained that 'the cacophony that surrounds us has become overwhelming'."
【句型】S + V + that + S + V ...
【核心說明】that 引導的名詞子句接在動詞後作為受詞，表達所知、所感或所說的內容。that 在此無意義，僅為連接作用。
【使用規則】
  • 常接 that 子句的動詞：know, believe, think, say, explain, hope。
  • 在口語中 that 常可省略。
  • 若主句動詞為過去式，子句通常也要改為過去式（時態一致）。
【主題例句】Experts say that white can reduce stress.（專家說白色可以減輕壓力。）
【學習提醒】不要把 that 引導的名詞子句與修飾名詞的形容詞子句混淆。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ I hope that you will happy.

✓ I hope that you will be happy.（that 子句內必須是完整句子）

試題：She realized ______ she had made a mistake. (A) what (B) that (C) which (D) whose

答案：(B)

【文法名稱】Infinitive 不定詞：表目的 (to / in order to)

【引句 Quote】
"...not simply to set a fashion trend, but to reflect the mood and needs..."
【句型】to + VR / in order to + VR / so as to + VR
【核心說明】不定詞可用來表達動作的目的，翻譯為「為了……」。在句中作為副詞修飾動詞。
【使用規則】
  • to + V：最常用，口語簡潔。
  • in order to + V：較正式，放在句首更具強調效果。
  • 否定目的：必須用 in order not to 或 so as not to。
【主題例句】She bought the book to learn about color theory.（她買這本書是為了學習色彩理論。）
【學習提醒】不可單獨使用 not to 表否定目的，會造成語意不清。
【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ He ran fast not to be late.

✓ He ran fast in order not to be late.（否定目的之固定用法）

試題：The artist used soft colors ______ create a sense of peace. (A) to (B) for (C) because (D) resulting in

答案：(A)
