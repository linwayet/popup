英文文章原文

原文 Original Article

More than any other event, the FIFA World Cup brings nations together through a shared language: football…

8句精讀

八句精讀 8 Key Sentence Analyses

①More than any other event

More than any other event, the FIFA World Cup brings nations together through a shared language: football.

世界盃足球賽比任何其他活動更能透過一種共同語言：足球，把各國凝聚在一起。

文法解析：這句使用比較級結構 more than any other + 單數名詞，用來表示「比任何其他……都更……」。主詞是 the FIFA World Cup，動詞 brings 搭配受詞 nations，再以 together 補充結果。through a shared language 表示方法或媒介。

bring ... together：凝聚shared language：共同語言more than any other：勝過其他任何

②分詞片語作開頭

Starting from humble beginnings, this stage has grown to become one of the most celebrated and watched sporting events in the world.

從樸實的起點開始，這個舞台已經成長為世界上最受讚揚、最受觀看的體育盛事之一。

文法解析：Starting from humble beginnings 是現在分詞片語，放在句首補充主詞 this stage 的發展背景。has grown 是現在完成式，強調從過去到現在的累積變化。one of the most + 形容詞 + 複數名詞表示「最……之一」。

humble beginnings：樸實起點has grown to become：已成長為celebrated：著名的

③並列句與時間背景

Back then, only 13 teams participated, and the tournament was held over just a few weeks.

當時只有 13 支隊伍參賽，而且整場賽事只在短短幾週內舉行。

文法解析：Back then 放句首，建立過去時間背景。and 連接兩個完整子句，前半為主動語態，後半 the tournament was held 為被動語態。over just a few weeks 表示活動持續的期間。

back then：當時participate：參與be held over：在……期間舉行

④Despite + 名詞片語

Despite the small start, the event laid the groundwork for what would become a global tradition.

儘管起步規模很小，這項活動仍為後來成為全球傳統的事物奠定了基礎。

文法解析：Despite 後面接名詞片語 the small start，表示「儘管……」。laid the groundwork for 是固定搭配，表示「為……奠定基礎」。what would become a global tradition 是名詞子句，作為介系詞 for 的受詞。

despite：儘管lay the groundwork for：奠定基礎global tradition：全球傳統

⑤though 引導讓步子句

That year, the tournament was held in Brazil—though Brazil famously lost the final match to Uruguay in front of nearly 200,000 fans at the Maracanã stadium in Rio de Janeiro, a shock that became known as the "Maracanazo."

那一年，賽事在巴西舉行，不過巴西卻在里約熱內盧馬拉卡納球場近二十萬名球迷面前，著名地在決賽輸給烏拉圭；這場震撼後來被稱為「馬拉卡納慘案」。

文法解析：though 引導讓步副詞子句，轉折說明雖然主辦地是巴西，結果卻由巴西輸掉決賽。a shock 是前面整件事的同位語，補充說明這個事件的性質。that became known as the "Maracanazo" 是形容詞子句，修飾 a shock。

famously lost：著名地落敗in front of：在……面前become known as：被稱為

⑥分詞構句補充背景

Making his debut at just 17 years old in the 1958 World Cup in Sweden, Pelé became the youngest player to score in a World Cup final.

貝利在瑞典 1958 年世界盃以年僅 17 歲之姿初登場，成為在世界盃決賽進球的最年輕球員。

文法解析：Making his debut... 是現在分詞構句，用來補充主詞 Pelé 的背景。became 後接名詞片語 the youngest player 作主詞補語。to score in a World Cup final 是不定詞片語，修飾 player；此處 to 是不定詞，不是介系詞。

make one's debut：首次登場at just 17：年僅 17 歲the youngest player to V：最年輕做某事的人

⑦分詞構句表示結果

He went on to win three World Cup titles with Brazil—in 1958, 1962, and 1970—cementing his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

他後來與巴西隊一起贏得三座世界盃冠軍，分別在 1958、1962 和 1970 年，進一步鞏固了他作為史上最偉大足球員之一的地位。

文法解析：went on to win 表示「接著、後來做某事」，to win 是不定詞。cementing his status 是現在分詞構句，表示前面贏得三座冠軍所帶來的結果。as one of the greatest footballers of all time 說明 status 的具體內容。

go on to V：後來繼續做cement one's status：鞏固地位of all time：有史以來

⑧Whether A or B

Whether you're a lifelong fan or a casual viewer, the road to 2026 promises to be one of the most exciting chapters in World Cup history yet.

無論你是一輩子的球迷，還是偶爾觀看的觀眾，通往 2026 的道路都注定會成為世界盃歷史中最令人興奮的篇章之一。

文法解析：Whether A or B 表示「無論是 A 或 B」，引導讓步語氣。promises to be 並不是「承諾要做」，在此表示「看來會是、可望成為」。one of the most exciting chapters 使用最高級結構，名詞 chapters 必須用複數。

lifelong fan：終身球迷casual viewer：偶爾觀看者promise to be：可望成為

文法解析

三個核心文法 Grammar Focus

一、分詞構句補充背景或結果

Making his debut at just 17 years old in the 1958 World Cup in Sweden, Pelé became the youngest player to score in a World Cup final.

V-ing ..., S + V ... / S + V ..., V-ing ...

核心說明：分詞構句常用來把兩個主詞相同或邏輯相關的句子合併，使句子更精簡。放在句首時，通常補充背景、時間或原因；放在句尾時，常補充結果或附帶狀況。

  • 主詞通常要和主要子句主詞一致，否則容易造成語意不清。
  • 現在分詞 V-ing 表主動；過去分詞 V-ed 常表被動。
  • 句首分詞構句後通常加逗號。
  • 若要表達明確時間，可保留 when、after、while 等連接詞。

主題例句：Winning three titles with Brazil, Pelé became a symbol of World Cup greatness.
（貝利與巴西隊贏得三座冠軍後，成為世界盃偉大的象徵。）

學習提醒：不要看到 V-ing 就只翻成「正在」。在分詞構句中，V-ing 的重點常是「主動關係」或「附帶狀況」，翻譯時要依上下文處理。

⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題
✗ Making his debut at 17, the final was unforgettable.
✓ Making his debut at 17, Pelé became a World Cup star.（分詞構句的邏輯主詞應是 Pelé。）
選擇：_____ three World Cup titles, Pelé became a legend. (A) Win (B) Winning (C) Won
答案：B

二、Despite + 名詞片語

Despite the small start, the event laid the groundwork for what would become a global tradition.

Despite + N/V-ing, S + V ...

核心說明：Despite 表示「儘管」，用來帶出與主要子句相反或出乎意料的背景。它是介系詞，不是連接詞，所以後面不能直接接完整的 S + V 子句。

  • Despite 後接名詞、名詞片語或動名詞。
  • 若要接完整子句，可寫 despite the fact that + S + V。
  • Although / Though 是連接詞，後面可直接接 S + V。
  • 句首 Despite 片語後通常加逗號。

主題例句：Despite its small beginning, the World Cup became a global sporting tradition.
（儘管起點很小，世界盃成為全球體育傳統。）

學習提醒：常見錯誤是寫成 despite it was difficult。因為 despite 是介系詞，後面若要放子句，必須改成 despite the fact that it was difficult，或直接用 although it was difficult。

⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題
✗ Despite Brazil lost the match, fans remembered the tournament.
✓ Although Brazil lost the match, fans remembered the tournament.（Although 可接完整子句。）
改錯：Despite the team was young, it played bravely.
答案：Despite being young, the team played bravely. / Although the team was young, it played bravely.

三、名詞子句 what + S + V

The event laid the groundwork for what would become a global tradition.

what + S + V = the thing(s) that + S + V

核心說明：what 引導名詞子句時，本身含有「……的事物」的意思。整個 what 子句可以當主詞、受詞或介系詞受詞；在原句中，what would become a global tradition 作介系詞 for 的受詞。

  • what 名詞子句不是疑問句，語序通常是 what + S + V。
  • what 已含有先行詞，不要再寫 the thing what。
  • 可放在介系詞後面，如 for what、about what、with what。
  • what 子句常用來強調核心概念。

主題例句：What surprised fans most was Brazil's loss in front of a huge home crowd.
（最讓球迷震驚的是巴西在龐大主場觀眾面前落敗。）

學習提醒：what 和 that 不同。that 名詞子句只負責連接，沒有「什麼事物」的意思；what 則在子句中扮演名詞角色，翻譯時常是「所……的事」。

⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題
✗ The event laid the groundwork for the thing what became a tradition.
✓ The event laid the groundwork for what became a tradition.（what 已包含 the thing that。）
選擇：_____ made Pelé special was his talent at a young age. (A) What (B) That (C) Which
答案：A

練文筆食材⌄

① More than any other + N（比任何其他……更……）
→ 適用：強調某事物的突出地位
例：More than any other sport, football connects people across cultures.

② Starting from + N, S + V（從……開始，……）
→ 適用：描述發展起點
例：Starting from a small club, the team became nationally famous.

③ one of the most + adj. + plural N（最……的……之一）
→ 適用：表達高度評價
例：The World Cup is one of the most watched events in the world.

④ be held in + place/year（在某地／某年舉行）
→ 適用：介紹活動背景
例：The final match was held in a packed stadium.

⑤ Despite + N/V-ing（儘管……）
→ 適用：表達轉折與對比
例：Despite heavy pressure, the young player scored the winning goal.

⑥ lay the groundwork for + N/V-ing（為……奠定基礎）
→ 適用：說明早期努力的重要性
例：Daily practice lays the groundwork for future success.

⑦ what + S + V（所……的事物）
→ 適用：強調核心結果或概念
例：What matters most is teamwork.

⑧ though + S + V（雖然……）
→ 適用：補充讓步或意外結果
例：The team played well, though it lost the match.

⑨ become known as + N（被稱為……）
→ 適用：介紹事件或稱號
例：The match became known as a turning point in football history.

⑩ Making one's debut..., S + V（初登場時……）
→ 適用：介紹人物首次亮相
例：Making her debut at sixteen, she surprised the audience.

⑪ go on to + V（後來繼續／接著……）
→ 適用：描述人物後續成就
例：He went on to become one of the best coaches in Asia.

⑫ cement one's status as + N（鞏固某人作為……的地位）
→ 適用：描述成就帶來的地位
例：The victory cemented her status as a top athlete.

⑬ from A to B（從 A 到 B）
→ 適用：呈現範圍或變化
例：Fans from Asia to Europe watched the final online.

⑭ Whether A or B, S + V（無論 A 或 B，……）
→ 適用：涵蓋不同讀者或情況
例：Whether you are a beginner or an expert, practice is essential.

⑮ promise to be + N/adj.（可望成為／看來會是……）
→ 適用：預測未來發展
例：The next tournament promises to be unforgettable.
