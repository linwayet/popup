Why We Can't Stop Loving Sanrio

品牌故事：為什麼我們無法停止愛上三麗鷗

林威老師與 AI agent 團隊製作

英文文章原文

Original Article

Why We Can't Stop Loving Sanrio Sanrio characters can be found everywhere, from school supplies to fashion items. Founded in Japan in 1960, the company didn't introduce its adorable characters until the 1970s. Since then, Sanrio has created over 450 characters, each with its own name and personality. While many brands fade away after a few years, Sanrio has stayed popular for decades.

What makes Sanrio characters so timeless? Their secret stems from their simple appearances. With soft colors, round shapes, and minimal details, these characters feel warm and friendly. Take Hello Kitty, for example. She has no visible mouth. Therefore, fans can imagine her conveying any emotion they feel. Happy, sad, or excited, she can be whatever you need.

And she can connect with diverse audiences. Sanrio characters also offer comfort during difficult times. Their world is peaceful and kind, focusing on friendship and everyday joys rather than drama or conflict. What's more, the brand appeals to people of all ages. Fans rarely outgrow Sanrio. Instead, they discover new favorites as their tastes and interests develop. A child might love the energetic Kuropi, while a teenager could relate to the rebellious Kuropi.

Every character possesses a personality that can match a person's individual style.

Aside from their cute appearances and personalities, many Sanrio characters have surprisingly detailed stories that deepen fans' connections to them. For example, Hello Kitty, whose real name is Kitty White, is described as a cheerful girl from London who loves baking cookies and making friends. Her gentle image has helped her become a global symbol of Sanrio. Then there is Cinema Roll, a white puppy born on a cloud.

His long ears work like wings and allow him to fly. One day, he came down from the sky and landed near Cafe Cinnamon, where the owner discovered him and took him in. Today, he spends his time having adventures with his friends. Pom Pom Purin, a laid-back golden retriever, represents a different lifestyle entirely. This pudding-loving pup enjoys long naps and wears a brown beret everywhere.

This pudding presents the relaxed, calm life many people dream of having. Sanrio keeps these characters fresh through regular updates. Seasonal merchandise brings characters into holidays and current trends, while social media accounts let fans interact with them daily. The annual character ranking, in which millions of fans vote for their favorites, creates excitement and community.

relevant and loved worldwide.

②句 2

文法解析：has created 是現在完成式，表示從過去到現在累積的成果。each with its own name and personality 是獨立片語，用 each 加介系詞片語補充說明每一個角色的特徵。這種寫法能讓句子更簡潔。

文法解析：While 在這裡引導讓步副詞子句，意思是「雖然」。主要子句使用現在完成式 has stayed，表示受歡迎的狀態從過去持續到現在。for decades 表示一段期間，所以常與完成式搭配。

文法解析

Grammar Focus

一、過去分詞片語作補充說明

【引句 Quote】
Founded in Japan in 1960, the company didn't introduce its adorable characters until the 1970s.

【句型】
Vpp + ..., S + V ...

【核心說明】
過去分詞片語放在句首時，常用來補充主詞的背景、原因或狀態。當主詞和動作之間是被動關係時，就使用過去分詞。

【使用規則】

  • 主詞是「被做某事」時，句首可用 Vpp。
  • 分詞片語的邏輯主詞必須和主要子句主詞一致。
  • 逗號用來分隔補充資訊和主要子句。
  • 若要強調時間，可保留 in 1960、after the meeting 等時間片語。

【主題例句】
Designed with simple shapes, the character became easy for children to remember.
（這個角色以簡單形狀設計，因此很容易讓孩子記住。）

【學習提醒】
不要看到句首 Vpp 就直接翻成「已經」。它通常不是完成式，而是省略了 which was / because it was 的分詞片語。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ Found in Japan in 1960, the company became famous.
✓ Founded in Japan in 1960, the company became famous.（公司是被創立，要用 founded。）

  • 選擇：___ in London, Kitty White loves baking cookies. (A) Describing (B) Described (C) Describe
    答案：B

二、關係子句修飾名詞

【引句 Quote】
Many Sanrio characters have surprisingly detailed stories that deepen fans' connections to them.

【句型】
N + that / which + V ...

【核心說明】
關係子句用來補充前面的名詞，讓名詞意思更完整。句中的 that deepen... 修飾 stories，說明這些故事有什麼功能。

【使用規則】

  • 先行詞是事物時，可用 that 或 which。
  • 限定用法不加逗號，因為子句是辨認名詞所必需的資訊。
  • 關係子句中的動詞要和先行詞單複數一致。
  • 若關係代名詞在子句中當受詞，常可省略。

【主題例句】
Fans enjoy characters that reflect their feelings and dreams.
（粉絲喜歡能反映自己感受與夢想的角色。）

【學習提醒】
先找先行詞，再判斷關係子句缺主詞還是受詞。這樣可以避免把 that 誤看成一般連接詞。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ Stories that deepens fans' connections are powerful.
✓ Stories that deepen fans' connections are powerful.（先行詞 stories 是複數，動詞用 deepen。）

  • 改錯：The ranking that create excitement is held every year.
    答案：create 改為 creates。

三、介系詞 + 關係代名詞：in which

【引句 Quote】
The annual character ranking, in which millions of fans vote for their favorites, creates excitement and community.

【句型】
N, prep. + which + S + V ..., V ...

【核心說明】
in which 是較正式的關係子句寫法，意思接近「在這個……裡」。句中 in which 指 in the annual character ranking，補充說明粉絲在這個排名活動中投票。

【使用規則】

  • 先判斷原本搭配的介系詞，例如 in the ranking、on the website、for the reason。
  • 介系詞提前後，後面通常用 which，不用 that。
  • 非限定子句前後用逗號，表示補充資訊。
  • 口語中常改寫為 where 或 which...in，但考試閱讀常見 in which。

【主題例句】
Sanrio runs online campaigns, in which fans share photos of their favorite characters.
（三麗鷗舉辦線上活動，粉絲在活動中分享最愛角色的照片。）

【學習提醒】
看到 in which 時，不要逐字翻成「在那一個」。先還原為 in + 前面的名詞，句意就會清楚。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ The ranking, in that fans vote, creates excitement.
✓ The ranking, in which fans vote, creates excitement.（介系詞後的關係代名詞不可用 that。）

  • 選擇：The cafe, ___ Cinnamoroll was discovered, became famous. (A) where (B) in that (C) which in
    答案：A；也可寫 in which。

練文筆食材⌄

① not ... until ...（直到……才……）
→ 適用：強調某事發生得較晚。
例：The company did not become famous until its characters appeared.

② Since then, S + have/has + Vpp（從那時起，……已經……）
→ 適用：描述從過去延續到現在的成果。
例：Since then, the brand has attracted fans worldwide.

③ While S + V, S + V（雖然……，但是……）
→ 適用：呈現對比。
例：While many trends disappear quickly, some characters remain popular.

④ What makes + O + adj.?（是什麼讓……如此……？）
→ 適用：引出分析主題。
例：What makes this mascot so memorable?

⑤ With + N, S + V（憑藉／帶有……，……）
→ 適用：描述特色帶來的效果。
例：With a simple design, the logo is easy to recognize.

⑥ imagine + O + V-ing（想像某人／物正在……）
→ 適用：描寫感受或投射。
例：Fans can imagine the character sharing their feelings.

⑦ rather than + N/V-ing（而不是……）
→ 適用：建立清楚對比。
例：The story focuses on friendship rather than conflict.

⑧ appeal to + N（吸引……）
→ 適用：分析品牌或作品的受眾。
例：The design appeals to people of different ages.

⑨ Aside from + N, S + V（除了……之外，……）
→ 適用：補充另一個重要理由。
例：Aside from cute images, the characters have rich stories.

⑩ N + that + V（……的名詞）
→ 適用：用關係子句補充名詞功能。
例：Students like stories that reflect their own dreams.

⑪ be described as + N/adj.（被描述為……）
→ 適用：介紹人物或角色設定。
例：The mascot is described as a brave young traveler.

⑫ allow + O + to V（允許／使……能夠……）
→ 適用：說明功能。
例：The simple face allows fans to imagine many emotions.

⑬ where + S + V（在那裡……）
→ 適用：補充地點資訊。
例：He landed near a cafe where the owner discovered him.

⑭ while + S + V（而……；同時……）
→ 適用：連接同時發生或對比的資訊。
例：Merchandise spreads the characters, while social media keeps fans involved.

⑮ in which + S + V（在其中……）
→ 適用：正式補充活動或情境。
例：The ranking, in which fans vote online, builds a strong community.
