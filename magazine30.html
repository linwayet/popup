A Tale of Three Chocolates
三種巧克力的奇幻旅程
林威老師與 AI Agent 團隊製作
【區塊一】英文文章原文

Whether dark, milk, or white, chocolate in all its many varieties is enjoyed around the world. But have you ever wondered what differences really exist between these types? The short answer lies in the ingredients. The ingredients can change the taste and appearance of chocolate. They can even have different effects on your body.

Starting with the type with the lowest cocoa content, white chocolate contains cocoa butter, but no cocoa solids. The lack of cocoa solids is what gives the chocolate its white color, and the butter gives it a creamy texture. White chocolate usually has a combination of vanilla, sugar, milk, and a few other ingredients.

Next, milk chocolate consists of at least 10% cocoa solids, as well as cocoa butter, sugar, and white chocolate. Milk chocolate is popular for having the perfect level of sweetness, and it's the color we normally associate with chocolate.

Finally, dark chocolate has many of the same ingredients as the other two types, but the biggest difference is the high cocoa solids concentration of over 35%. Unlike the other two types, it has little to no milk solids. The combination of high cocoa and low milk levels gives it a delightfully bitter taste. It's a rich color.

Beyond chocolate's wonderful taste, it can also provide some health benefits. In general, the more cocoa solids chocolate contains, the more nutritious it is, largely due to compounds called polyphenols. Dark chocolate has the most cocoa solids, therefore it has the most polyphenols. That's why dark chocolate has been linked to improved blood flow, which may support the skin, the brain, and other vital organs.

Milk chocolate has a medium amount of cocoa solids, so it has some of these benefits. It also contains small amounts of caffeine and compounds that can provide temporary energy. However, eating too much milk chocolate can contribute to health issues because of its sugar levels. Since it has no cocoa solids, white chocolate is an excellent choice for people who want to avoid caffeine. On the other hand, white chocolate is high in sugar and fat, which can cause a lot of health problems. Which makes it the least healthy option overall.

All kinds of chocolate are tasty, but they don't all have health benefits. Chocolate with plenty of cocoa solids is the healthiest, and some studies even suggest that it can help fight diabetes and lower stress. So, grab some dark chocolate and prepare to make your belly, and the rest of your body, happy.

【區塊四】文法解析

【文法名稱】Whether... or...（無論是...還是...）

【引句 Quote】 Whether dark, milk, or white, chocolate in all its many varieties is enjoyed around the world.
【句型】 Whether A, B, or C, S + V...
【核心說明】

這是一個引導「讓步副詞子句」的用法，表示不論在何種情況下，主句的內容都成立。它可以連接單字、片語或完整的子句。

【使用規則】
  • 連接兩項時：Whether A or B.
  • 連接多項時：Whether A, B, or C.
  • 後接子句時：Whether S + V or not.
  • 注意：與 whether 引導的名詞子句（是否）不同，讓步子句位於句首時常用逗號與主句隔開。
【主題例句】

Whether it is expensive or cheap, you should check the ingredients before buying chocolate.
（無論它是昂貴還是便宜，買巧克力前都應該檢查成分。）

【學習提醒】

在表示「無論」時，whether 常用於對比兩者或多者；而 no matter how/what 則強調程度或內容。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Either it rains or shines, we will go on a picnic.
✓ Whether it rains or shines, we will go on a picnic. (Either用於選擇其一，Whether用於讓步)

題目：________ you like it or not, we have to finish this project by Friday.
答案：Whether

【文法名稱】What 引導的名詞子句（...的事物）

【引句 Quote】 The lack of cocoa solids is what gives the chocolate its white color.
【句型】 S + be + what + S + V / V...
【核心說明】

這裡的 what 是關係代名詞，本身包含了先行詞（the thing that），引導一個名詞子句。這個子句可以在主句中當主詞、受詞或補語。

【使用規則】
  • what = the thing(s) that。
  • what 後接不完整的子句（缺主詞或受詞）。
  • 當主詞時，動詞通常用單數。
【主題例句】

What makes dark chocolate healthy is its high concentration of polyphenols.
（讓黑巧克力健康的原因是它高濃度的多酚。）

【學習提醒】

注意不要與 that 引導的名詞子句混淆；that 後面接完整的子句，what 後面接不完整的子句。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ I don't know that he wants.
✓ I don't know what he wants. (wants 缺受詞，故用 what)

題目：________ surprised me the most was the different effects of these chocolates.
答案：What

【文法名稱】The + 比較級..., the + 比較級...（愈...就愈...）

【引句 Quote】 The more cocoa solids chocolate contains, the more nutritious it is.
【句型】 The + adj/adv比較級 (+ S + V), the + adj/adv比較級 (+ S + V)
【核心說明】

這個句型用來表示兩個情況成正比的變化。前一個 the 引導的是副詞子句（條件），後一個 the 引導的是主句（結果）。

【使用規則】
  • 比較級後可以接名詞（The more money...）。
  • 主詞和動詞如果是 be 動詞或根據上下文可推知，常可省略。
  • 注意逗號的使用。
【主題例句】

The dark chocolate is, the more polyphenols it has.
（巧克力愈黑，含有的多酚就愈多。）

【學習提醒】

比較級前的 the 不可省略，這是此特殊句型的特徵。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ More you study, better your grades will be.
✓ The more you study, the better your grades will be.

題目：The ________ (early) you start eating healthy, the ________ (good) you will feel.
答案：earlier; better

✍️ 練文筆食材▼

① Whether A, B, or C...（無論是 A, B, 還是 C...）
→ 適用：窮舉情況，強調主句的一致性。
例：Whether you are a student or a teacher, you need to keep learning.

② Have you ever wondered...?（你是否曾想過...？）
→ 適用：文章開頭，引發讀者好奇心。
例：Have you ever wondered how the internet works?

③ Starting with...（從...開始）
→ 適用：有順序地介紹多個項目時。
例：Starting with the most important point, we need to focus on safety.

④ The lack of... is what gives...（...的缺失正是賦予...的原因）
→ 適用：解釋特徵的成因。
例：The lack of practice is what makes him fail the test.

⑤ Be popular for + V-ing...（因...而受歡迎）
→ 適用：描述事物的優點。
例：Taiwan is popular for having many delicious snacks.

⑥ In general...（一般來說）
→ 適用：下概括性的結論。
例：In general, exercising daily is good for your health.

⑦ The + 比較級..., the + 比較級...（愈...愈...）
→ 適用：描述比例關係。
例：The harder you work, the luckier you get.

⑧ largely due to...（主要歸功於/由於...）
→ 適用：說明主要原因。
例：The project's success was largely due to team cooperation.

⑨ Be linked to...（與...有關聯）
→ 適用：描述因果或相關性。
例：Lack of sleep is often linked to high stress levels.

⑩ On the other hand...（另一方面）
→ 適用：表達對比或相反觀點。
例：Online learning is convenient; on the other hand, it requires self-discipline.
