【區塊一】英文文章原文

A suspension bridge is a type of bridge where the road is hung from thick steel cables that stretch between tall towers. These main cables are fixed firmly at both ends of the bridge in huge blocks of concrete or rock called anchorages, which help keep the whole structure in balance. Because of this special design, suspension bridges are lighter than many other bridges and can cross very wide rivers, bays, or valleys with just a few supports in the water.

Some of the most famous bridges in the world use this design. The Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco is known for its orange color and beautiful art deco style, and it has become a symbol of the United States. In Japan, the Akashi Kaikyō Bridge was long considered the world’s longest suspension bridge and shows advanced engineering that can resist strong earthquakes and typhoons. More recently, Turkey’s 1915 Çanakkale Bridge became the longest suspension bridge span, connecting the European and Asian sides of the country and carrying thousands of vehicles every day.

Suspension bridges are not just convenient roads; they are also engineering wonders and popular tourist spots. Their long spans make travel faster by cutting down driving time between important cities and regions. At the same time, their elegant shapes and impressive height often inspire photographers, filmmakers, and travelers from around the world. For many people, walking or driving across a world-famous suspension bridge is a special experience that combines daily transportation with views they will never forget.

【區塊五】文法解析

1. 形容詞子句：where / that / which

【引句 Quote】

A suspension bridge is a type of bridge where the road is hung from thick steel cables that stretch between tall towers.

【句型】

N + where + S + V / N + that or which + V...

【核心說明】

形容詞子句用來修飾前面的名詞，也就是先行詞。where 常修飾地點或有位置概念的名詞，that / which 則常修飾事物，並可在子句中作主詞或受詞。

本句中 where 修飾 bridge，說明道路懸掛的位置；that 修飾 cables，說明鋼纜會延伸在高塔之間。

【使用規則】

  • where = in / at / on which，常用於地點或空間概念。
  • that 可修飾人或物，但通常不用在非限定子句中。
  • which 修飾事物，可用在限定或非限定子句。
  • 若關係代名詞在子句中作受詞，有時可以省略。

【主題例句】

The tower that supports the cables must be extremely strong.
（支撐鋼纜的塔必須非常堅固。）

【學習提醒】

不要看到地點名詞就一定用 where；如果關係詞在子句中作主詞或受詞，仍要用 which 或 that。例如 the bridge that crosses the river 中，that 是 crosses 的主詞。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ The bridge where crosses the bay is famous.
✓ The bridge that crosses the bay is famous.（關係詞作 crosses 的主詞，要用 that / which。）

選擇：The city ______ the bridge stands attracts many tourists.
A. which　B. where　C. what
答案：B

2. 不定詞表目的：to + V

【引句 Quote】

Engineers use strong materials to help the bridge resist earthquakes and typhoons.

【句型】

S + V + O + to + V...

【核心說明】

to + 原形動詞可表示目的，意思是「為了……」。這裡的 to 是不定詞符號，不是介係詞，所以後面接原形動詞，不接 V-ing。

文章談到橋梁能抵抗地震與颱風，若改寫成目的句，就可用 to help the bridge resist... 表示工程設計的目的。

【使用規則】

  • to 表目的時，後面接原形動詞。
  • 較正式可用 in order to + V 或 so as to + V。
  • 否定目的常用 in order not to + V。
  • 不要把不定詞 to 和介係詞 to 混淆；look forward to 後面才接 V-ing。

【主題例句】

Workers check the cables regularly to keep the bridge safe.
（工人定期檢查鋼纜，以保持橋梁安全。）

【學習提醒】

表目的的不定詞常放在主要動作之後，回答「為了什麼」。若句子已經有 because of 表原因，不要誤把原因和目的混成同一個結構。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Engineers designed the bridge to resisting earthquakes.
✓ Engineers designed the bridge to resist earthquakes.（to 是不定詞，後面用原形動詞。）

改錯：The city built a new bridge to reducing traffic.
答案：reducing → reduce

3. 現在分詞片語補充說明

【引句 Quote】

Turkey’s 1915 Çanakkale Bridge became the longest suspension bridge span, connecting the European and Asian sides of the country and carrying thousands of vehicles every day.

【句型】

S + V..., V-ing... and V-ing...

【核心說明】

現在分詞片語可以放在主要子句後面，補充說明主詞的動作、結果或伴隨狀況。這種寫法常見於說明文，能把資訊濃縮在同一句中。

本句的 connecting 和 carrying 都補充說明 1915 Çanakkale Bridge 的功能：它連接兩側，也承載車輛。

【使用規則】

  • 分詞片語的邏輯主詞通常要和主要子句主詞一致。
  • 現在分詞 V-ing 表主動或正在進行的概念。
  • 過去分詞 V-ed 表被動或完成的概念。
  • 分詞片語放在句尾時，常用逗號與主要子句分開。
  • 若主詞不清楚，容易造成修飾對象錯誤。

【主題例句】

The bridge rises above the river, offering visitors a beautiful view.
（這座橋矗立在河上，提供遊客美麗景色。）

【學習提醒】

使用分詞片語時，要先確認分詞動作是誰做的。若句首或句尾的 V-ing 找不到合理主詞，句子就會變得不自然或有文法問題。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Walking across the bridge, the view was unforgettable.
✓ Walking across the bridge, we found the view unforgettable.（walking 的主詞應是人，不是 view。）

選擇：The new bridge opened last year, ______ two towns across the valley.
A. connect　B. connecting　C. connected
答案：B
