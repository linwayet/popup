Hot Day in Taiwan? Time for Some Ice Desserts!

一口一口吃進夏天：探索臺灣冰品的魅力

林威老師與 AI agent 團隊製作

英文文章原文

Original Article

Eating cold desserts is a simple way to cool off during summer in Taiwan. Dessert shops and street stands across the island sell many kinds of cool snacks. One comes as fine ice with add-ons you choose. Another mixes warm toppings with ice in the same bowl. Still another is a thick, traditional ice cream that melts slowly and feels extra smooth.

Among these, baobing, or shaved ice, is especially common in Taiwan. It is made by scraping a block of ice into a soft, snowy hill. Some people add red beans, mung beans, or peanuts for a nutty bite. Others add grass jelly or pudding for a soft texture. Still others top their ice with fruits like mangoes or strawberries when they are in season. After adding the ingredients, many people pour sweet extras like syrup, brown sugar, or condensed milk on top.

Whether served as a wonderful quick snack or a shared dessert after a meal, baobing can be as simple or as rich as you like. Even after you finish, the gentle chill stays with you, keeping the lingering summer heat at bay.

Meanwhile, if you enjoy surprises, hot-and-cold ice may be just the thing for you. It combines two temperatures in one bowl, serving freshly cooked toppings paired with a pile of ice. As you eat, the ice melts and blends with the warm toppings, creating a pleasant contrast. It is also fun because the flavor changes as the bowl warms up. Many people prefer this on cooler days, when a fully cold dessert might feel like too much.

Another classic is babu ice cream, a traditional Taiwanese ice cream. Made with sugar and starch, babu ice cream has a rich texture with flavors ranging from taro and peanut to pineapple. It stays firm longer than regular ice cream, making it easier to eat in the heat. Street vendors often serve it in a cone or a small cup. Either way, it is a convenient snack to enjoy on the go. For many people, it also brings back childhood memories and a sense of nostalgia.

Together, baobing, hot-and-cold ice, and babu ice cream give you three fun ways to beat the heat. Try them all, and you may find a new summer favorite!

8 Key Sentence Analyses

①句 1

Eating cold desserts is a simple way to cool off during summer in Taiwan.

吃冰涼的甜點是在臺灣夏天消暑的一個簡單方法。

文法解析：本句以動名詞片語 Eating cold desserts 作主詞，後面接單數動詞 is。to cool off 是不定詞片語，修飾 a simple way，表示「用來消暑的方法」。這裡的 to 是不定詞符號，不是介係詞。

cool off 消暑during summer 在夏天期間

②句 2

Still another is a thick, traditional ice cream that melts slowly and feels extra smooth.

還有另一種是濃厚的傳統冰淇淋，融化得慢，而且口感特別滑順。

文法解析：that melts slowly and feels extra smooth 是形容詞子句，修飾前面的 ice cream。子句中有兩個並列動詞 melts 和 feels，共同描述這種冰淇淋的特色。extra 在此作副詞，修飾形容詞 smooth。

traditional 傳統的extra smooth 特別滑順

③句 3

Among these, baobing, or shaved ice, is especially common in Taiwan.

在這些冰品當中，刨冰在臺灣尤其常見。

文法解析：Among these 是介系詞片語，放在句首用來限定討論範圍。or shaved ice 是同位語，用另一種說法補充說明 baobing。主詞仍是 baobing，所以動詞用單數 is。

among these 在這些之中shaved ice 刨冰

④句 4

Still others top their ice with fruits like mangoes or strawberries when they are in season.

還有一些人會在冰上加上像芒果或草莓這類當季水果。

文法解析：top A with B 是動詞片語，意思是「在 A 上加 B」。like mangoes or strawberries 舉例說明 fruits 的種類。when they are in season 是時間副詞子句，說明這些水果在當季時最常被使用。

top A with B 在 A 上加 Bin season 當季

⑤句 5

Whether served as a wonderful quick snack or a shared dessert after a meal, baobing can be as simple or as rich as you like.

不論是作為美好的快速點心，或是餐後共享的甜點，刨冰都可以依你喜好變得簡單或豐富。

文法解析：Whether served as... 是省略主詞與 be 動詞的讓步副詞子句，完整概念可理解為 Whether it is served as...。主要子句是 baobing can be...。as simple or as rich as you like 使用 as...as 結構，表示程度可以符合你的喜好。

whether...or... 不論是…或…as rich as you like 依你喜好一樣豐富

⑥句 6

It combines two temperatures in one bowl, serving freshly cooked toppings paired with a pile of ice.

它把兩種溫度結合在同一碗裡，端上新鮮煮好的配料，並搭配一堆冰。

文法解析：本句主要動詞是 combines。逗號後的 serving... 是現在分詞構句，補充說明 hot-and-cold ice 如何呈現。paired with a pile of ice 是過去分詞片語，修飾 toppings，表示「與一堆冰搭配」。

combine A with B 結合 A 與 Bpaired with 搭配

⑦句 7

It is also fun because the flavor changes as the bowl warms up.

它也很有趣，因為味道會隨著碗逐漸變暖而改變。

文法解析：because the flavor changes 是原因副詞子句，說明它有趣的原因。as the bowl warms up 是時間副詞子句，這裡的 as 表示「隨著」。同一句中使用 because 與 as，分別表原因與時間，閱讀時要依語意判斷。

flavor 味道warm up 變暖

⑧句 8

Made with sugar and starch, babu ice cream has a rich texture with flavors ranging from taro and peanut to pineapple.

叭噗冰淇淋由糖和澱粉製成，質地濃厚，口味從芋頭、花生到鳳梨都有。

文法解析：Made with sugar and starch 是過去分詞構句，表示被動含義，補充說明 babu ice cream 的製作材料。with flavors ranging from...to... 是介系詞片語，當中 ranging 修飾 flavors，表示口味範圍。from A to B 是常見的範圍結構。

made with 由…製成range from A to B 從 A 到 B 不等

文法解析

Grammar Focus

動名詞片語作主詞

【引句 Quote】
Eating cold desserts is a simple way to cool off during summer in Taiwan.

【句型】
V-ing + 單數動詞 + 補語

【核心說明】
當一個動作被當成「事情」來談時，可以用動名詞 V-ing 當主詞。這種主詞通常視為單一概念，因此後面的動詞多用單數。

【使用規則】

  • V-ing 當主詞表示「做某事」這件事。
  • 整個 V-ing 片語當主詞時，動詞通常用單數。
  • 若要表目的，可在名詞後接 to V，例如 a way to cool off。
  • to V 中的 to 是不定詞符號，不是介係詞。

【主題例句】
Trying different ice desserts is a fun way to learn about Taiwanese food culture.
（嘗試不同冰品是認識臺灣飲食文化的有趣方法。）

【學習提醒】
中文常把「吃冰」放在句首，但英文若要把動作當主詞，不可直接用原形動詞，應改成 V-ing 或 To V。高中考題常測主詞和動詞一致。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ Eat cold desserts are a simple way to cool off.
✓ Eating cold desserts is a simple way to cool off.（V-ing 作主詞，動詞用 is。）

  1. ____ local snacks helps visitors understand a city. (A) Try (B) Trying (C) Tried
    答案：B
  2. 改錯：Buying fresh fruit are easy in Taiwan.
    答案：are → is

分詞構句補充說明

【引句 Quote】
It combines two temperatures in one bowl, serving freshly cooked toppings paired with a pile of ice.

【句型】
S + V..., V-ing...

【核心說明】
現在分詞構句可以放在主要子句後面，補充說明同一主詞所做的附帶動作、結果或方式。此處 serving 說明 hot-and-cold ice 如何把兩種溫度呈現在同一碗裡。

【使用規則】

  • 分詞構句的邏輯主詞通常要和主要子句主詞一致。
  • V-ing 多表示主動或同時發生的動作。
  • 過去分詞 paired with 表被動，修飾 toppings。
  • 若語意不清，應改寫成完整子句。

【主題例句】
The vendor served the shaved ice in a paper bowl, adding mangoes and syrup on top.
（攤販把刨冰裝在紙碗裡，並在上面加入芒果和糖漿。）

【學習提醒】
分詞構句最常見的錯誤是主詞不一致。例如「走進店裡，冰品看起來很好吃」英文不能讓 dessert 當作 walking 的主詞。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ Walking into the shop, the baobing looked delicious.
✓ Walking into the shop, I thought the baobing looked delicious.（走進店裡的人是 I。）

  1. The ice melted slowly, ____ with the warm toppings. (A) blending (B) blended (C) to blend
    答案：A
  2. 改錯：Waiting in line, the dessert shop smelled sweet.
    答案：可改為 Waiting in line, we smelled the sweet dessert shop.

as 引導時間副詞子句

【引句 Quote】
It is also fun because the flavor changes as the bowl warms up.

【句型】
S + V + as + S + V

【核心說明】
as 可以引導時間副詞子句，表示「當…時」或「隨著…」。本句中 as 強調碗逐漸變暖的同時，味道也跟著變化。

【使用規則】

  • as 表時間時，常帶有「同步變化」的感覺。
  • because 表原因，as 也可表原因，但本句 because 已清楚標示原因。
  • as + S + V 是完整子句，不可只接單一名詞。
  • 若要接名詞，可用 as a student 這類「作為…」的不同用法。

【主題例句】
As the ice melts, the brown sugar mixes with the milk and becomes sweeter.
（隨著冰融化，黑糖和牛奶混合在一起，味道變得更甜。）

【學習提醒】
看到 as 不要只背一個意思。它可能表示「當…時」「因為」「如同」「作為」，考試時要看前後句意。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ The flavor changes as warming up the bowl.
✓ The flavor changes as the bowl warms up.（as 後面接完整子句。）

  1. ____ the dessert warmed up, the flavor became richer. (A) As (B) So (C) Because of
    答案：A
  2. 改錯：As the ice melting, the syrup spread through the bowl.
    答案：melting → melted

練文筆食材⌄
① a simple way to V（做某事的簡單方法）
→ 適用：介紹方法或解決方案時
例：Drinking enough water is a simple way to stay healthy.
② across + 地點（遍及…）
→ 適用：描述範圍很廣的現象
例：Night markets across Taiwan attract many visitors.
③ One..., another..., still another...（一個…另一個…還有一個…）
→ 適用：列舉三種選項或例子
例：One student chose music, another chose art, and still another chose science.
④ be made by V-ing（藉由…製成）
→ 適用：說明製作方式
例：This dessert is made by mixing tea with milk.
⑤ top A with B（在 A 上加 B）
→ 適用：描述食物配料
例：You can top the toast with honey and bananas.
⑥ Whether..., ...（不論…，…）
→ 適用：強調兩種情況都成立
例：Whether eaten hot or cold, the snack tastes great.
⑦ as simple or as rich as you like（可依喜好簡單或豐富）
→ 適用：描述彈性選擇
例：Your travel plan can be as simple or as rich as you like.
⑧ keep + O + at bay（阻止…靠近）
→ 適用：寫健康、壓力或問題控制
例：Regular exercise helps keep stress at bay.
⑨ may be just the thing for you（可能正適合你）
→ 適用：推薦選項
例：If you like quiet places, this library may be just the thing for you.
⑩ combine A with B（把 A 與 B 結合）
→ 適用：描寫創新、混合特色
例：The course combines language learning with cultural activities.
⑪ creating a pleasant contrast（形成令人愉快的對比）
→ 適用：描述設計、食物、文章轉折
例：The sweet sauce and salty cheese create a pleasant contrast.
⑫ ranging from A to B（範圍從 A 到 B）
→ 適用：列出種類範圍
例：The club offers activities ranging from hiking to photography.
⑬ making it easier to V（使做某事更容易）
→ 適用：說明結果或好處
例：Online maps make it easier to explore a new city.
⑭ Either way, S + V（不論哪種方式，…）
→ 適用：收束兩種可能情況
例：You can study alone or with friends. Either way, review is important.
⑮ bring back childhood memories（喚起童年回憶）
→ 適用：寫食物、音樂、地方與懷舊感
例：The old song brought back childhood memories for my father.
