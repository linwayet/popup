中譯英分析

題 1 原答案

More and more senior high sclool teachers have increased the proportion of using English during class.

  • 優點：句子主幹完整，More and more...、現在完成式方向正確。
  • 問題：sclool 拼字錯；「在課堂上使用英文的百分比」較自然是 the percentage/proportion of English used in class 或 the amount of English they use in class。
建議答案：More and more senior high school English teachers have increased the percentage of English they use in class.
題 2 原答案

They divide students into different groups with their ability of English, taking various activities of litsening and speaking.

  • 優點：divide students into different groups 架構正確。
  • 問題：「依能力」要用 according to / based on；ability of English 應改為 English ability/proficiency；活動搭配要用 carry out / conduct / do activities，不是 take activities；listening 拼字錯。
建議答案：They divide students into different groups according to their English ability and carry out various listening and speaking activities.

翻譯程度判斷：目前能寫出基本句型，但介系詞、名詞搭配、動詞搭配與拼字會扣分。若要衝非選分數，最有效的練法是「中文句子切主幹 → 固定搭配 → 檢查詞性與拼字」。
