【區塊一】英文文章原文 Original Article

Sanrio characters can be found everywhere, from school supplies to fashion items. Founded in Japan in 1960, the company didn't introduce its adorable characters until the 1970s. ...

What makes Sanrio characters so timeless? Their secret stems from their simple appearances. With soft colors, round shapes, and minimal details, these characters feel warm and friendly. Take Hello Kitty, for example. She has no visible mouth. Therefore, fans can imagine her conveying any emotion they feel. Happy, sad, or excited, she can be whatever you need.

And she can connect with diverse audiences. Sanrio characters also offer comfort during difficult times. Their world is peaceful and kind, focusing on friendship and everyday joys rather than drama or conflict. What's more, the brand appeals to people of all ages. Fans rarely outgrow Sanrio. Instead, they discover new favorites as their tastes and interests develop. A child might love the energetic Kuropi, while a teenager could relate to the rebellious Kuropi.

Every character possesses a personality that can match a person's individual style.

Aside from their cute appearances and personalities, many Sanrio characters have surprisingly detailed stories that deepen fans' connections to them. For example, Hello Kitty, whose real name is Kitty White, is described as a cheerful girl from London who loves baking cookies and making friends. Her gentle image has helped her become a global symbol of Sanrio. Then there is Cinema Roll, a white puppy born on a cloud.

His long ears work like wings and allow him to fly. One day, he came down from the sky and landed near Cafe Cinnamon, where the owner discovered him and took him in. Today, he spends his time having adventures with his friends. Pom Pom Purin, a laid-back golden retriever, represents a different lifestyle entirely. This pudding-loving pup enjoys long naps and wears a brown beret everywhere.

This pudding presents the relaxed, calm life many people dream of having. Sanrio keeps these characters fresh through regular updates. Seasonal merchandise brings characters into holidays and current trends, while social media accounts let fans interact with them daily. The annual character ranking, in which millions of fans vote for their favorites, creates excitement and community.

relevant and loved worldwide.

【區塊二】八句精讀 8 Key Sentence Analyses
1
Sanrio characters can be found everywhere, from school supplies to fashion items.

三麗鷗角色隨處可見，從學校用品到時尚單品應有盡有。

文法解析：

句型結構：被動語態 (Passive Voice) + 範圍介系詞 (from... to...)。本句使用 can be found 強調角色「被發現」的普遍性，而非主動去尋找。from... to... 則用來列舉角色滲透生活的廣泛範圍。

supplies 生活用品 fashion items 時尚單品 everywhere 隨處
2
Founded in Japan in 1960, the company didn't introduce its adorable characters until the 1970s.

該公司於 1960 年在日本創立，但直到 1970 年代才推出其可愛的角色。

文法解析：

句型結構：分詞構句 (Participle Phrase) + Not... until 句型。Founded in... 是過去分詞引導的構句，修飾主詞 the company（公司是被創立的）。didn't... until... 表「直到……才……」，是強調時間點的重要句型。

founded 創立 adorable 可愛的 introduce 介紹/推出
3
While many brands fade away after a few years, Sanrio has stayed popular for decades.

雖然許多品牌在幾年後便逐漸消失，但三麗鷗卻能數十年維持高人氣。

文法解析：

句型結構：讓步副詞子句 (While) + 現在完成式 (Present Perfect)。While 在此表示「雖然、儘管」，用來對比其他品牌與三麗鷗的差異。has stayed 表從過去持續到現在的狀態。

fade away 消失 stay popular 維持受歡迎 decades 數十年
4
Therefore, fans can imagine her conveying any emotion they feel.

因此，粉絲可以想像她正在傳達他們所感受到的任何情緒。

文法解析：

句型結構：感官/想像動詞受詞補語 (imagine + O + Ving) + 形容詞子句省略。imagine her conveying 表示「想像她正在傳達」；(that) they feel 則修飾 emotion，省略了受格關係代名詞。

therefore 因此 conveying 傳達 emotion 情緒
5
Instead, they discover new favorites as their tastes and interests develop.

相反地，隨著品味和興趣的發展，他們會發現新的心頭好。

文法解析：

句型結構：轉折副詞 (Instead) + 時間/比例副詞子句 (as)。Instead 用來承接前句的否定（不會長大就變心）。as 引導子句，表示「隨著」兩個動作同時發展的比例關係。

instead 相反地 tastes 品味 develop 發展
6
Aside from their cute appearances and personalities, many Sanrio characters have surprisingly detailed stories that deepen fans' connections to them.

除了可愛的外表和個性，許多三麗鷗角色還有令人驚訝的詳細故事，加深了粉絲與他們之間的連結。

文法解析：

句型結構：介系詞片語 (Aside from) + 關係代名詞 (that) 引導的限定子句。Aside from 等於 In addition to（除了……之外還有）。that deepen... 則是修飾 stories 的形容詞子句。

aside from 除了...之外 surprisingly 令人驚訝地 deepen 加深
7
One day, he came down from the sky and landed near Cafe Cinnamon, where the owner discovered him and took him in.

有一天，他從天而降，降落在肉桂咖啡館附近，那裡的店主發現了他並收留了他。

文法解析：

句型結構：關係副詞 (where) 引導的非限定子句。where 用來修飾地點 Cafe Cinnamon，在此等於 and there，補充說明在該地點發生的後續動作。

landed 降落 discovered 發現 took him in 收留他
8
The annual character ranking, in which millions of fans vote for their favorites, creates excitement and community.

一年一度的角色排名賽（數百萬粉絲在其中投票選出最愛）創造了興奮感與社群意識。

文法解析：

句型結構：介系詞 + 關係代名詞 (Prep + Which)。in which 引導非限定形容詞子句修飾 ranking。這種寫法比單用 which 更正式，清楚表達了投票發生在「排名賽活動之中」。

annual 一年一度的 ranking 排名 community 社群/共同體

【區塊四】文法解析 Grammar Analysis

【文法名稱】Adverbial Clause of Reason 副詞子句：原因子句 (because / since / as)

【引句 Quote】

While many brands fade away after a few years, Sanrio has stayed popular for decades because they discover new favorites as their tastes and interests develop.

【句型】

Because + S + V ..., S + V ...

【核心說明】

原因副詞連接詞引導的子句說明主要動作的原因或理由。本文中 because 引導的子句說明了三麗鷗能長紅的原因。as 則在此句中表示「隨著」，說明時間的比例關係。

【使用規則】

  • because：原因最直接，語氣最強，常用於回答 why。
  • since：由於（說話雙方已知的原因，較正式）。
  • as：因為（語氣較弱，常置句首）。

【主題例句】

Sanrio characters are popular because they offer comfort during difficult times.（三麗鷗角色之所以受歡迎，是因為他們在艱難時期提供了慰藉。）

【學習提醒】

because of 是介系詞，後面只能接名詞或代名詞，不可接完整子句。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Because of Hello Kitty has no mouth, fans imagine her emotions.
✓ Because Hello Kitty has no mouth, fans imagine her emotions.

選擇題：Sanrio characters are popular ________ they have simple appearances. A. because of B. since C. as to D. despite
答案：B。說明：此處接子句，since 可表原因。

【文法名稱】Relative Clause 形容詞子句：who / which / that

【引句 Quote】

Aside from their cute appearances and personalities, many Sanrio characters have surprisingly detailed stories that deepen fans' connections to them.

【句型】

N + that/which/who + V ...

【核心說明】

形容詞子句用來修飾前面的名詞（先行詞）。本句中 that deepen... 限定修飾 stories，說明這些故事的功能是「加深粉絲的連結」。

【使用規則】

  • that 常用於限定子句，前面通常沒有逗號。
  • which 用於修飾物；who 用於修飾人。
  • 非限定用法（補充說明）前面要加逗號，且不能使用 that。

【主題例句】

Hello Kitty, whose real name is Kitty White, is from London.（凱蒂貓（其真名為 Kitty White）來自倫敦。）

【學習提醒】

whose 是關係代名詞的所有格，後面必須接名詞（如 whose name）。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ The puppy, that was born on a cloud, is called Cinnamoroll.
✓ The puppy, which was born on a cloud, is called Cinnamoroll.（逗號後不接 that。）

選擇題：The character ________ millions of fans love is Hello Kitty. A. who B. whom C. which D. whose
答案：B。說明：受格關係代名詞指人（Kitty 被愛），可用 whom 或 who/that。

【文法名稱】Participle Phrase 分詞句構：副詞子句化簡法

【引句 Quote】

Founded in Japan in 1960, the company didn't introduce its adorable characters until the 1970s.

【句型】

V-ing / V-pp ..., S + V ...

【核心說明】

當副詞子句的主詞與主要子句相同時，可省略連接詞與主詞，將動詞改為分詞。本文中的 Founded... 是由 (As it was) founded... 化簡而來，因為公司是被創立的，故使用過去分詞 V-pp。

【使用規則】

  • 主動關係：使用現在分詞 V-ing。
  • 被動關係：使用過去分詞 V-pp。
  • 當 be 動詞化為 being 時，通常予以省略。

【主題例句】

Focusing on friendship, the world of Sanrio is peaceful.（由於專注於友誼，三麗鷗的世界非常和平。）

【學習提醒】

檢查分詞句構時，一定要把主詞帶回去分詞處，看動作是主動還是被動。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Finding in 1960, the company is old.
✓ Founded in 1960, the company is old.（公司是被發現/創立的。）

選擇題：________ on a cloud, the white puppy is very light. A. Born B. Bearing C. Beared D. To bear
答案：A。說明：puppy 是「被出生」的（born）。

折疊四：練文筆食材▼
① not only... but also...（不只…而且…）
→ 適用：強調雙重優點或問題時
例：Not only does Sanrio create cute characters, but it also builds a strong community.
② aside from + N/V-ing（除了…之外）
→ 適用：增加額外資訊或優點
例：Aside from Hello Kitty, Cinnamoroll is another global favorite.
③ what + S + V（名詞子句當主詞）
→ 適用：強調核心論點
例：What makes Sanrio characters timeless is their simple design.
④ not... until...（直到...才...）
→ 適用：強調特定時間點的轉變
例：The company didn't introduce its mascot until it had a clear identity.
⑤ rather than...（而不是...）
→ 適用：對比兩者並強調其中之一
例：Focus on kindness rather than conflict.
⑥ as... develop（隨著...發展）
→ 適用：描述隨時間變化的趨勢
例：As technology develops, Sanrio finds new ways to interact with fans.
⑦ whose real name is...（其真名為...）
→ 適用：補充說明背景細節
例：Kitty White, whose real name is Hello Kitty, is a global icon.
⑧ can be whatever you need（可以是你需要的任何樣子）
→ 適用：形容事物的多樣性或適應性
例：This tool can be whatever you need for your project.
⑨ deepen the connection（加深連結）
→ 適用：描述情感或關係的增強
例：Stories help deepen the connection between fans and characters.
⑩ remain relevant（維持相關性/不退流行）
→ 適用：描述品牌的持久力
例：Constant updates help the brand remain relevant in a changing world.
