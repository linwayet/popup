Unit 1｜單字複習

先看搭配，再背中文

昀軒的弱點不是完全看不懂文章，而是容易被相近單字和局部語意干擾。先把單字放回常見搭配裡，效果會比單背中文好。

Unit 2｜閱讀填充練習

主題：地圖、旅程與文化街區

Reading Passage: Directions Are Never Just Directions

When Lian visited Stone Bay, a small indigenous district on Taiwan's eastern coast, she expected a simple tourist stop. Instead, she found a place where tradition coexists with change. An antique shop sold oil lamps and wooden mirrors, while a modern studio displayed aboriginal necklaces made by local artists. The neighborhood was not trying to become a crowded tourist mecca; it wanted visitors to notice its cultural diversity and buy meaningful souvenirs.

At the visitor center, Lian saw an unusual map. Its orientation changed according to each walking route. A guide explained that map layouts have never been neutral. In some pre-biblical traditions, east was placed at the top; in old Islamic maps, south could be placed above north because early Muslims lived north of Mecca. Later, European exploration and imperialism made many maps more Eurocentric. The modern north-up habit, the guide said, might be only a historical blip, not an eternal rule.

Lian then climbed a narrow spiral staircase inside a medieval-style museum. The stairs turned clockwise, and the guide asked visitors to think like castle defenders. Attackers had to ascend one at a time and could easily trip on uneven steps. Defenders, familiar with the pattern, could retreat upward quickly. This design favored right-handed defenders and made a mass attack difficult.

The day ended with a short film about a famed polar explorer and a sunken ship. The crew's voyage was slowed when ice pushed the ship northward and left it trapped. Stormy seas and bad weather hindered rescue attempts, but the team eventually reached a whaling station. For Lian, the lesson was clear: whether we read maps, history, or exam passages, we must follow clues carefully and avoid hasty conclusions.

學測提醒

針對昀軒錯題
1. 看搭配
designer 前面是 amateur；concerns 前面是 grave；consumption 才能和食用量搭配。
2. 回文找指涉
第 40 題 them 要回到前一句找可複數指涉的名詞，不靠印象猜。
3. 推論抽象詞
blip 可從 not a rule 推出「短暫現象」，不要只看字面陌生就亂選。
4. 檢查輸出
one of a kind 少 a 會扣分；be blended with 需要過去分詞。

建議練習順序

第一輪先讀 Unit 1 的用法欄，遮住中文回想搭配。第二輪做 Unit 2，練從文章語境判斷答案。第三輪做 Unit 3，練學測單字題的干擾選項。最後把錯題整理成短語：grave concerns、balanced consumption、dread the thought of V-ing、be blended with、one of a kind。
