【區塊一】英文文章原文

英文文章原文 Original Article

Penguins are stars of the southern hemisphere. While they're famous for their cute appearance and smooth swimming, these birds have a very strange habit. They swallow pebbles on purpose.

These pebbles serve several purposes in a penguin's life. One of them is to help penguins digest food, which often contains hard bones or shells. Once swallowed, the stones move into a special part of the penguin's stomach and help grind up the food. This makes it easier for penguins to break down their meals and get the most nutrients out of every bite.

Pebbles may also help penguins dive. Penguins have body fat and air trapped in their feathers. These increase their buoyancy. By swallowing a few pebbles, the birds can add weight, dive faster, and stay underwater longer.

These pebbles, however, do not stay in their stomachs for good. They gradually wear down and are passed out of the body.

Unfortunately, as ocean pollution gets worse, many penguins mistake plastic trash for pebbles, leading them to swallow it by accident. To save these amazing birds, we must protect the waters they depend on. Keeping their habitat clean is essential for their long-term survival.

【區塊四】文法解析

三個核心文法點 Grammar Focus

【文法名稱】Infinitive of Purpose 不定詞表目的

【引句 Quote】

One of them is to help penguins digest food, which often contains hard bones or shells.

【句型】

S + V + to + 原形動詞 + O

【核心說明】

不定詞 to V 可以用來表示目的，中文常譯為「為了……」。在本文中，to help penguins digest food 說明企鵝吞石子的用途之一。

注意 to 是不定詞標記，不是介系詞；因此後面要接原形動詞 help，而不是 helping。

【使用規則】

  • to + V 是最常見的目的用法。
  • in order to + V 較正式，適合書面或演講。
  • so as to + V 較書面，常用於正式文章。
  • 否定目的常用 in order not to 或 so as not to。

【主題例句】

Penguins swallow pebbles to digest food more easily.
（企鵝吞下小石子，是為了更容易消化食物。）

【學習提醒】

看到 to 後面接原形動詞時，要先判斷它是否表示目的。若前面是動作，後面的 to V 很常回答「為了什麼」。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Penguins swallow pebbles to digesting food.

✓ Penguins swallow pebbles to digest food.

說明：to 是不定詞標記，後面必須接原形動詞 digest。

選出正確句：A. Penguins use stones to grinding food. B. Penguins use stones to grind food. C. Penguins use stones for grind food. D. Penguins use stones to ground food.

答案：B。說明：to + 原形動詞 grind 表示目的。

【文法名稱】Participial Construction 分詞構句

【引句 Quote】

Unfortunately, as ocean pollution gets worse, many penguins mistake plastic trash for pebbles, leading them to swallow it by accident.

【句型】

S + V ..., V-ing ...

【核心說明】

分詞構句可以補充主要子句的結果、原因、時間或附帶動作。本文中的 leading them to swallow it by accident 說明「誤認塑膠垃圾」造成的結果。

當 V-ing 放在主要子句後面時，常可翻成「結果造成……」或「並且……」。

【使用規則】

  • 現在分詞 V-ing 多表示主動或自然結果。
  • 過去分詞 V-ed 多表示被動或完成狀態。
  • 分詞構句的邏輯主詞要能從主要子句清楚判斷。
  • 若句意不清，應改回完整子句。

【主題例句】

Plastic trash looks like pebbles, causing penguins to make dangerous mistakes.
（塑膠垃圾看起來像小石子，導致企鵝犯下危險的錯誤。）

【學習提醒】

分詞構句雖然簡潔，但不要把主詞關係寫亂。若不確定誰做了分詞動作，就改用 and、because 或 which 子句會更安全。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Penguins mistake plastic for pebbles, lead them to swallow it.

✓ Penguins mistake plastic for pebbles, leading them to swallow it.

說明：逗號後補充結果時，動詞 lead 要改成現在分詞 leading。

改寫句子：Penguins swallow plastic by accident, and this harms their health.

答案：Penguins swallow plastic by accident, harming their health. 說明：harming 補充前面動作造成的結果。

【文法名稱】Gerund as Subject 動名詞當主詞

【引句 Quote】

Keeping their habitat clean is essential for their long-term survival.

【句型】

V-ing + O/C + singular verb + complement

【核心說明】

動名詞片語可以當整個句子的主詞，表示一件事情或一個行為。Keeping their habitat clean 是主詞，後面搭配單數動詞 is。

這種句型常用於寫作主題句，能把行動直接放在句首，讓重點更清楚。

【使用規則】

  • 動名詞當主詞時，動詞通常用單數。
  • 動名詞片語可包含受詞、補語或副詞。
  • keep + O + adj. 表示「使……保持某狀態」。
  • 若主詞太長，也可改成 It is essential to keep their habitat clean.

【主題例句】

Reducing ocean pollution protects penguins from plastic trash.
（減少海洋污染能保護企鵝免受塑膠垃圾危害。）

【學習提醒】

中文常說「保持棲地乾淨是重要的」，英文可以直接用 Keeping ... is important。不要把句首 V-ing 誤判成現在進行式，因為它在這裡是名詞功能。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】

✗ Keep their habitat clean is essential.

✓ Keeping their habitat clean is essential.

說明：句首動作當主詞時，要用動名詞 Keeping。

選出正確句：A. Protect the ocean are necessary. B. Protecting the ocean is necessary. C. To protecting the ocean is necessary. D. Protected the ocean is necessary.

答案：B。說明：Protecting the ocean 是動名詞片語當主詞，動詞用 is。

文章相關常用片語 5 個

• be famous for（以……聞名）

• on purpose（故意地）

• grind up（磨碎）

• mistake A for B（把 A 誤認為 B）

• depend on（依賴；取決於）

折疊四：練文筆食材 ⌄
① While S + V, S + V（雖然……，但是……）
→ 適用：用來鋪陳反差或轉折。
例：While penguins look cute, they have surprising survival habits.
② be famous for + N/V-ing（以……聞名）
→ 適用：介紹特色、人物或地點。
例：Taiwan is famous for its friendly people and night markets.
③ on purpose（故意地）
→ 適用：強調某動作不是偶然發生。
例：The student left early on purpose to avoid the crowd.
④ help + O + V（幫助某人／某物做……）
→ 適用：說明功能、效果或用途。
例：Regular exercise helps students reduce stress.
⑤ which + V（非限定形容詞子句補充說明）
→ 適用：在主句後補充整件事的影響。
例：Plastic waste enters the sea, which harms many animals.
⑥ Once + V-ed, S + V（一旦被……，就……）
→ 適用：描述流程或事件順序。
例：Once recycled, plastic bottles can become new products.
⑦ make it + adj. + for + O + to V（使某人做某事變得……）
→ 適用：說明某因素造成的便利或困難。
例：Online tools make it easier for students to review lessons.
⑧ by + V-ing（透過……）
→ 適用：提出方法、手段或解決方案。
例：We can protect oceans by reducing plastic waste.
⑨ as + S + V（隨著……）
→ 適用：描述同步變化或趨勢。
例：As technology improves, learning becomes more flexible.
⑩ mistake A for B（把 A 誤認為 B）
→ 適用：描述誤判、混淆或辨識錯誤。
例：Tourists sometimes mistake this building for a museum.
⑪ leading + O + to V（導致某人／某物去做……）
→ 適用：說明結果或後果。
例：Too much screen time affects sleep, leading students to feel tired.
⑫ To save + O, S + must + V（為了拯救……，我們必須……）
→ 適用：提出行動呼籲。
例：To save endangered animals, people must protect their habitats.
⑬ depend on + N（依賴……）
→ 適用：說明生存、成功或結果所依靠的條件。
例：Many families depend on clean rivers for drinking water.
⑭ Keeping + O + adj. is essential for + N（保持……對……很重要）
→ 適用：寫環保、健康或公共議題的主題句。
例：Keeping public spaces clean is essential for community health.
⑮ long-term + N（長期的……）
→ 適用：討論長期影響、計畫或結果。
例：Good sleep habits support long-term academic success.
