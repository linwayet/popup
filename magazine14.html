【區塊二】八句精讀

8 Key Sentence Analyses
1 If you have been following pop culture or watching Netflix recently, you've almost certainly heard the song Golden.
如果您最近一直有關注流行文化或收看 Netflix，您幾乎肯定聽過《Golden》這首歌。
文法解析：本句使用了第一類條件句（Conditional Type 1），並結合了現在完成進行式（have been following）來強調動作的持續性。
pop culture 流行文化
almost certainly 幾乎肯定地
2 It is the powerful anthem from the 2025 animated movie K-pop Demon Hunters, sung by the character Rumi and her K-pop girl group Huntrix.
它是 2025 年動畫電影《K-pop 惡魔獵人》中強而有力的主題曲，由角色 Rumi 及其 K-pop 女子團體 Huntrix 演唱。
文法解析：sung by... 是過去分詞片語，用來修飾前面的名詞 anthem，相當於 which is sung by... 的簡化。
powerful anthem 強而有力的頌歌
animated movie 動畫電影
3 The young girl from Seoul joined SM Entertainment... as a trainee with the dream of debuting in a girl group.
這位來自首爾的小女孩加入 SM 娛樂……成為一名練習生，夢想著在女子團體中出道。
文法解析：of debuting 為介系詞後接動名詞的用法，作為 dream 的同位語，說明夢想的內容。
trainee 練習生
debut 出道
4 There, she spent 10 years practicing singing and dancing for long hours as she waited for her moment to take the stage.
在那裡，她花了 10 年時間練習歌唱和舞蹈，長時間等待著登上舞台的時刻。
文法解析：使用了 spend + 時間 + V-ing 的句型。as 在此作為連接詞，表示「當……的時候」。
take the stage 登上舞台
for long hours 長時間地
5 The choice to continue her studies helped EJ discover that she could express herself more freely through songwriting.
選擇繼續深造幫助 EJ 發現，她可以透過作曲更自由地表達自己。
文法解析：help + 受詞 + (to) V 是使役動詞用法。that 引導名詞子句作為 discover 的受詞。
express oneself 表達自己
freely 自由地
6 Interestingly, EJ's life closely paralleled that of her character, Rumi.
有趣的是，EJ 的生活與她的角色 Rumi 息息相關。
文法解析：that of her character 中的 that 用來指代前面的單數名詞 life，避免重複。
parallel 與...平行/相似
that of ... 的那個
7 Fans fell in love not only with Rumi, but also with EJ.
歌迷們不僅愛上了 Rumi，也愛上了 EJ。
文法解析：使用了 not only A but also B 的對等連接詞句型，在此連結兩個介系詞片語。
fall in love with 愛上...
not only ... but also 不僅...而且
8 EJ, who had experienced the frustration of rejection years earlier, was performing on stage and in television shows.
EJ 早年曾歷經被拒絕的挫折，現在則是在舞台上和電視節目中表演。
文法解析：who had experienced... 是非限定用法形容詞子句，對主詞 EJ 進行補充說明。過去完成式（had experienced）強調發生在表演動作之前。
frustration 挫折
experience 經驗/經歷

【區塊四】文法解析

Grammar Analysis

1. 副詞子句：條件子句（if / even if）

If you have been following pop culture or watching Netflix recently, you've almost certainly heard the song Golden.

句型：If + S + V ..., S + V ...

核心說明：條件子句用來表示主要子句動作成立的前提。本篇文章中使用了 If 引導的基本條件句，以及 even if 引導的「即使……」讓步條件句。

使用規則：在條件子句中，常用現在式代替未來式。

主題例句：If you keep trying, another door will open for you.

⚠️ 常見錯誤：✗ If it will rain tomorrow, I will stay at home. ✓ If it rains tomorrow, I will stay at home. 選擇題：Even if you are tired, you must finish your work. 答案：Even if.

2. 形容詞子句：who / which / that

EJ, who had experienced the frustration of rejection years earlier, was performing on stage.

句型：N + who/which/that + V ...

核心說明：形容詞子句（關係子句）用來修飾前面的名詞。本句使用了非限定用法（有逗號），對 EJ 進行額外的補充說明。

使用規則：非限定用法（有逗號）中不能使用 that，只能用 who 或 which。

⚠️ 常見錯誤：✗ EJ, that was a trainee for 10 years, finally became famous. ✓ EJ, who was a trainee for 10 years, finally became famous.

3. 動名詞常見片語：spend + time + V-ing

There, she spent 10 years practicing singing and dancing for long hours.

句型：S + spend + time/money + V-ing

核心說明：spend 後接時間或金錢時，其後的動作必須使用動名詞（V-ing）形式。

主題例句：EJ spent a decade developing her music production skills.

⚠️ 常見錯誤：✗ She spent three hours to finish her homework. ✓ She spent three hours finishing her homework.

✍️ 折疊四：練文筆食材▼
① not only A but also B（不僅 A 而且 B）— 例：EJ is not only a talented singer but also a skilled songwriter.
② every bit as ... as ...（完全就像...一樣）— 例：Her journey is every bit as inspiring as the characters she plays.
③ step away from ...（離開...；退出...）— 例：She decided to step away from the trainee path and seek a new direction.
④ express oneself more freely through ...（透過...更自由地表達自己）— 例：Many artists find they can express themselves more freely through abstract art.
⑤ parallel that of ...（與...的相似/平行）— 例：The story of the movie closely paralleled that of her own life.
⑥ launch into the spotlight（推向大眾焦點）— 例：The viral video launched the small-town singer into the spotlight.
⑦ When one door closes, another often opens（天無絕人之路）— 例：EJ's success reminds us that when one door closes, another often opens.
