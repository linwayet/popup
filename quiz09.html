衍生詞intricately (adv. 精細地)；intricacy (n. 複雜性；錯綜之處)

學習第二外語需要在文法、詞彙與文化之間取得精細的平衡。
🔴 不熟悉

修復；恢復；復原
衍生詞restore (v. 修復；恢復)；restorative (adj. 恢復性的)；restorer (n. 修復者)
字根sta / st 站立；穩定 → stand 站；stable 穩定；establish 建立
片語process of restoration 修復過程；under restoration 修復中；careful restoration 細心修復

③ renowned [rɪˈnaʊnd] adj.
著名的；享有盛名的
衍生詞renown (n. 聲望；盛名)；world-renowned (adj. 舉世聞名的)
片語be renowned for 以……聞名；renowned artist 著名藝術家；internationally renowned 國際知名的
容易混淆renowned 較正式，含「受人尊敬」語感；famous 一般性著名；noted 強調因特定特質被注意；celebrated 強調被讚揚

巴黎聖母院的大鐘 Emmanuel，以其和諧優美的音色聞名於世。

這座城市以其宏偉的中世紀建築和充滿活力的街頭文化聞名。

她在發表了有關古代文物的突破性研究後，成為一位享譽學界的知名研究員。

艾菲爾鐵塔是全球參觀人數最多的古蹟之一，每年吸引數百萬遊客。
②This ancient monument stands as a reminder of the civilization that built it over a thousand years ago.

🔵 同等級
⑤ heritage [ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ] n.
文化遺產；遺留下來的傳統
衍生詞inherit (v. 繼承)；inheritance (n. 繼承；財產)；inheritor (n. 繼承人)
片語cultural heritage 文化遺產；world heritage site 世界遺產地；national heritage 國家文化遺產
容易混淆heritage 指文化或歷史遺留的整體；inheritance 多指個人繼承的財產或特質；legacy 強調留給後代的影響
①The ancient town was listed as a UNESCO world heritage site in 1987.
這座古老城鎮在 1987 年被列為聯合國教科文組織的世界遺產地。
②Protecting our cultural heritage means not only preserving buildings, but also languages and traditions.
保護我們的文化遺產不只是保存建築物，也包括保存語言與傳統習俗。
③The museum's collection is part of the national heritage that future generations will inherit.
這座博物館的館藏是未來世代將繼承的國家文化遺產的一部分。
🔵 同等級
⑥ preserve [prɪˈzɜrv] v. / n.
保存；保護；維持（原貌）
衍生詞preservation (n. 保存；維護)；preservative (n./adj. 防腐劑；保存性的)；well-preserved (adj. 保存完好的)
片語preserve traditions 保存傳統；preserve food 保存食物；preserve wildlife 保護野生動物；in good preservation 保存良好
容易混淆preserve 強調防止損壞或流失；protect 著重防禦外來威脅；conserve 強調節約使用；maintain 強調維持現有狀態

保存古老遺跡讓後代子孫也能親身感受，是我們的責任。
②The cold, dry climate of the region helped to preserve the artifacts found inside the ancient tomb.
該地區乾冷的氣候有助於保存在古墓內發現的文物。
③Local volunteers worked hard to preserve the traditional customs and folk songs of their village.
當地志工努力保存村莊的傳統習俗和民歌。

⑦ ancient [ˈeɪnʃənt] adj. / n.
古老的；古代的；（the ancients）古人
衍生詞the ancients (n. 古人；前人)；anciently (adv. 在古代)
片語ancient civilization 古代文明；ancient ruins 古代遺址；ancient history 古代史；in ancient times 在古代
容易混淆ancient 指很久遠的年代（通常千年以上）；antique 指古老且有收藏價值的物品；old 是一般性描述

埃及金字塔是人類雙手建造過最令人歎為觀止的古代建築之一。
②Archaeologists discovered an ancient city buried under layers of desert sand.
考古學家在一層層的沙漠沙丘下發現了一座被掩埋的古城。
③Studying ancient languages helps us understand how people lived and thought thousands of years ago.
研究古代語言有助於我們了解數千年前的人們如何生活與思考。

⑧ disaster [dɪˈzæstər] n.
災難；災禍；慘敗
衍生詞disastrous (adj. 災難性的；極其糟糕的)；disastrously (adv. 災難性地)

容易混淆disaster 指嚴重不幸事件；catastrophe 語氣最強，指毀滅性大災難；accident 指一般意外事故；tragedy 強調悲劇性

2019 年巴黎聖母院的大火是一場震驚全世界的文化災難。

國際救援隊迅速被派往，為洪水災民提供救災援助。

⑨ significant [sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt] adj.
重要的；有意義的；顯著的

片語of great significance 意義重大；a significant achievement 重要成就；significantly different 顯著不同

當考古學家在市場下方發現一幅被掩埋的古代馬賽克時，取得了重大發現。

失去一座歷史古蹟的意義，不只對某個國家重大，對全人類來說皆然。

📐Part 2｜文法加強 3 大重點
依據 Alice 在 114 學測 + 高二段考中最常錯的題型設計，例句關鍵字以粗體標示，英中對照
① have difficulty / trouble + V-ing（做某事有困難）

錯誤來源：高二段考 Q35 — Alice 選了 to find，正解是 finding

✅ have difficulty / trouble / fun / a hard time  (in) + V-ing
❌ have difficulty to + V（不定詞不可用在此句型）
📌 同類固定句型：spend time V-ing（花時間做）、have fun V-ing（開心做）、it's worth V-ing（值得做）。記住：這四個句型統統接 V-ing，不接 to V。
Tourists often have difficulty understanding the local dialect when visiting small towns.
遊客在造訪小鎮時，常常很難聽懂當地方言。
Art restorers had great trouble recreating the intricate patterns on the ancient mosaic.
藝術修復師在重建古代馬賽克上精細複雜的圖案時，遭遇了極大的困難。
Many people have a hard time imagining what life was like before modern technology existed.
許多人很難想像在現代科技出現之前，生活是什麼樣子。
② be noted / renowned / known / famous for（以……聞名）

錯誤來源：114 學測 Q21 — Alice 選了 familiar，但 be familiar 應接 with（對……熟悉）

✅ be noted / renowned / known / famous + for + N / V-ing（以……聞名）
✅ be familiar + with + N（對……熟悉）← 注意介系詞不同！
❌ be familiar for（錯誤用法，not a thing）
📌 配對記憶：noted/renowned/known/famous → 接 for；familiar → 接 with。考試看到 be ___ for，選項絕不能選 familiar！
Notre-Dame is noted for its intricate architecture, stunning windows, and, above all, its bells.
巴黎聖母院以其精細建築、令人驚豔的玻璃窗，尤其是鐘聲聞名。
The professor is renowned for her significant contributions to the study of ancient civilizations.
這位教授以她對古代文明研究的重大貢獻而享有盛名。
After years of study, she became familiar with the intricate process of art restoration.
經過多年學習，她對藝術修復那複雜的過程已相當熟悉。（be familiar with = 熟悉）
③ where 引導關係副詞子句（地點先行詞 + 完整子句）

錯誤來源：高二段考 Q34 — Alice 選了 which，但後面子句完整，應用關係副詞 where

✅ 地點名詞 + where + 完整子句（主詞 + 動詞 都有）
✅ 地點名詞 + which + 不完整子句（缺少受詞時才用 which）
判斷法：把 where/which 帶入後，子句是否還缺一個名詞？缺 → which；不缺 → where
📌 快速口訣：where = 在那裡（副詞）；子句自己有完整的主詞和受詞 → 選 where。
例：He visited the city where he was born.（he was born 完整 → where）
例：He visited the city which he loves.（he loves __ 缺受詞 → which）
He returned to the museum where the ancient artifacts were first discovered.
他回到了最初發現那些古代文物的博物館。（the ancient artifacts were first discovered 子句完整 → where）
Paris is a city where remarkable monuments stand on almost every street corner.
巴黎是一座幾乎每個街角都矗立著令人讚嘆古蹟的城市。
The library where she had studied for years was listed as a significant heritage site.
她求學多年的那座圖書館，被列為重要的文化遺產保護地。
📖Part 3｜閱讀測驗
主題與 Day 1 單字高度相關 · 長度約 115 年學測閱讀 1/2 · 3 題選擇
The Living Past
~ 115 words · 約學測閱讀 1/2 長度 ~

Every year, millions of tourists travel to see the world's most renowned heritage sites. Ancient monuments such as the Colosseum in Rome and the Angkor Wat temples in Cambodia are not simply ruins — they are living connections to the civilizations that created them. However, preserving these significant structures is never simple.

Natural disasters, pollution, and human activity all threaten to destroy what centuries have carefully protected. When disaster strikes, restoration becomes essential. The process of restoring a damaged monument can be intricate, taking years or even decades. Yet for many communities, the effort is worth every penny.

Having survived the test of time, these monuments remind us of our shared human heritage. They carry intricate stories written not in books, but in stone.

1. What is the main idea of this passage?
  • Ancient buildings are too expensive to repair and should be left as ruins.
  • Heritage sites connect us to our past and are worth the effort to preserve.
  • Tourism is harmful to ancient monuments and must be strictly controlled.
  • Natural disasters are the single greatest threat to ancient civilizations.
點此查看答案 ▼
✔ 正解：(B)
全文強調遺址是「與過去的聯繫」，且「值得投入心力保存」。注意末段：「remind us of our shared human heritage」— 這才是全文核心。(A)(C)(D) 都只是細節或未提及。
2. According to the passage, why does restoration sometimes take decades?
  • Governments often disagree on how to fund restoration projects.
  • Tourism needs to be halted completely during the restoration process.
  • The process of restoring a monument can be intricate and time-consuming.
  • Restoration materials are extremely difficult to find in modern times.
點此查看答案 ▼
✔ 正解：(C)
第二段明確說：「The process of restoring a damaged monument can be intricate, taking years or even decades.」直接對應選項 (C)。注意：本題考細節定位，務必回文找到對應句。
3. The word "intricate" (paragraph 3) is closest in meaning to:
  • simple and easy to follow
  • complex and full of fine detail
  • short and quickly forgotten
  • ancient and historically distant
點此查看答案 ▼
✔ 正解：(B)
intricate = 錯綜複雜的；精細的 → closest to "complex and full of fine detail"。注意：(A) 是反義；(D) 是 ancient 的意思，不要混淆。
✏️Part 4｜單字單選複習 5 題
考驗今日 10 個單字的掌握程度 · 完成後按下方按鈕一次查看全部答案與解析
1. The artist is ______ for the intricate details she carves into each wooden sculpture.
  • familiar
  • noted
  • suited
  • related
2. After the flood, the city launched a five-year ______ project to rebuild its ancient temples.
  • disaster
  • heritage
  • restoration
  • monument
3. Scientists have difficulty ______ how the builders of ancient monuments moved such enormous stones.
  • to explain
  • explain
  • explained
  • explaining
4. The town ______ she grew up has been added to the national heritage list.
  • which
  • whom
  • where
  • that
5. Surprisingly, the ancient bell ______ the revolution and still rings in the cathedral today.
  • restored
  • preserved
  • survived
  • inherited
📋 點此查看答案 & 解析  ｜  手機版請調正 ⌄
第 1 題 ✔ 正解：(B) noted
be noted for = 以……聞名（固定搭配）。
❌ (A) familiar 應接 with（be familiar with）不接 for — 這正是學測 Q21 的錯誤！
❌ (C) suited 搭配 for，語意為「適合」，與「聞名」無關。
第 2 題 ✔ 正解：(C) restoration
restoration project = 修復計畫；restore v. 修復。
(A) disaster = 災難（rebuild 之前是 disaster 發生，但空格說的是 rebuild 的動作 → restoration）
(D) monument 是「古蹟」名詞，不能當 project 的修飾詞（需改為 monumental project）。
第 3 題 ✔ 正解：(D) explaining
have difficulty + V-ing 是固定句型，不可用不定詞（to explain）。
❌ (A) to explain — 高二段考 Q35 Alice 就是選了這個錯誤選項！
口訣：have difficulty / trouble / fun / a hard time → 全部接 V-ing。
第 4 題 ✔ 正解：(C) where
先行詞 = the town（地點），後面子句「she grew up」主詞 + 動詞完整，不缺受詞 → 用關係副詞 where。
❌ (A) which — which 後面子句需缺少受詞（如：the town which she loves）
❌ (D) that — that 不可用於非限定子句（此處有逗號或地點副詞語意）
第 5 題 ✔ 正解：(C) survived
survive + 困難/災難 = 從……倖存。「熬過革命」→ survived the revolution。
❌ (A) restored = 被修復（被動語態應為 was restored）
❌ (B) preserved = 保存（preserved 後通常接物品被動保存，不接 the revolution 這種事件）
❌ (D) inherited = 繼承（不接 the revolution）
