英文文章原文

Original Article

Weird but true. Unbelievable facts from the animal kingdom. The animal kingdom is full of surprises that challenge our understanding of biology. From remarkable feeding mechanisms to extreme defense strategies, some creatures possess traits so unusual that they seem like pure fiction. Here are four fascinating examples that show nature's endless creativity. The humble snail may appear simple, but its mouth tells a different story. Snails possess a ribbon-like tongue called a radula, densely covered with anywhere from 1,000 to 12,000 tiny teeth. This structure works like a cheese grater, allowing them to scrape and grind food with surprising efficiency. Far from being passive creatures, snails are actually well-equipped eating machines. Squids face an unusual challenge. where esophagus runs directly through their brain. Consequently, swallowing prey that is too large, such as fragments of crab shell, can potentially cause brain damage. This peculiar arrangement forces squid to thoroughly break down their food before swallowing, making every meal a careful operation. Deep in African rainforests, the hairy frog has developed one of nature's most extreme defense mechanisms. When threatened, this creature deliberately fractures its own toe bones and forces them through the skin to form sharp claws, earning comparisons to the comic book character Wolverine. How these frogs recover from such self-inflicted injuries remains a scientific mystery. Finally, hippos often appear to sweat blood, but this striking red secretion is actually a natural protective fluid. It functions as both sunscreen against intense African heat and an antibacterial agent keeping wounds clean. Taken together, these facts are a reminder, evolution doesn't aim for elegance, it aims for survival, even when the result looks wonderfully weird.

8 Key Sentence Analyses

①句 1

The animal kingdom is full of surprises that challenge our understanding of biology.

動物王國充滿了挑戰我們對生物學理解的驚奇事實。

文法解析：be full of 表示「充滿……」。that challenge... 是關係子句，修飾 surprises。challenge 在此不是「挑戰比賽」，而是「使某種理解受到考驗」。
animal kingdom 動物王國challenge 挑戰biology 生物學

②句 2

From remarkable feeding mechanisms to extreme defense strategies, some creatures possess traits so unusual that they seem like pure fiction.

從非凡的進食機制到極端的防衛策略，有些生物擁有異常到像純屬虛構的特徵。

文法解析：From A to B 用來呈現範圍。so + adj. + that... 表示「如此……以至於……」。possess 是較正式的「擁有」，常用於描述特徵或能力。
mechanism 機制defense strategy 防衛策略fiction 虛構

③句 3

Snails possess a ribbon-like tongue called a radula, densely covered with anywhere from 1,000 to 12,000 tiny teeth.

蝸牛擁有一條像緞帶般、稱為齒舌的舌頭，上面密密麻麻覆蓋著 1,000 到 12,000 顆小牙齒。

文法解析：called a radula 是過去分詞片語，修飾 tongue。densely covered with... 也是分詞片語，補充說明齒舌的狀態。anywhere from A to B 表示「從 A 到 B 不等」。
radula 齒舌densely covered 密集覆蓋anywhere from 從……到……不等

④句 4

This structure works like a cheese grater, allowing them to scrape and grind food with surprising efficiency.

這個構造像起司刨絲器一樣運作，讓牠們能以驚人的效率刮取並磨碎食物。

文法解析：works like... 用比喻說明功能。allowing them to... 是現在分詞片語，補充主要動作帶來的結果。to scrape and grind 中的 to 是不定詞符號，後接兩個平行動詞。
structure 構造scrape and grind 刮取並磨碎efficiency 效率

⑤句 5

Consequently, swallowing prey that is too large, such as fragments of crab shell, can potentially cause brain damage.

因此，吞下過大的獵物，例如螃蟹殼碎片，可能會造成腦部損傷。

文法解析：Consequently 表示「因此」，承接前文的原因。句子的主詞是動名詞片語 swallowing prey...。that is too large 是關係子句，修飾 prey。
consequently 因此prey 獵物potentially 可能地

⑥句 6

This peculiar arrangement forces squid to thoroughly break down their food before swallowing, making every meal a careful operation.

這種奇特的構造迫使魷魚在吞嚥前徹底分解食物，使每一餐都成了一項小心翼翼的行動。

文法解析：force + O + to V 表示「迫使某人／某物做……」，to 是不定詞。before swallowing 中 before 是介係詞，後面接 V-ing。making... 是分詞構句，補充前面情況造成的結果。
peculiar 奇特的thoroughly 徹底地operation 行動；操作

⑦句 7

When threatened, this creature deliberately fractures its own toe bones and forces them through the skin to form sharp claws, earning comparisons to the comic book character Wolverine.

受到威脅時，這種生物會故意折斷自己的趾骨，並迫使骨頭穿出皮膚形成銳利爪子，因此被拿來和漫畫角色金鋼狼比較。

文法解析：When threatened 是省略句，完整概念是 when it is threatened。to form sharp claws 是不定詞表目的。earning comparisons... 是現在分詞片語，補充說明這種行為帶來的結果。
deliberately 故意地fracture 折斷comparison 比較

⑧句 8

Taken together, these facts are a reminder, evolution doesn't aim for elegance, it aims for survival, even when the result looks wonderfully weird.

整體來看，這些事實提醒我們：演化的目標不是優雅，而是生存，即使結果看起來奇妙又怪異。

文法解析：Taken together 是過去分詞片語，表示「合在一起看」。後面用對比結構 doesn't aim for..., it aims for... 強調演化的目標。even when 引導讓步副詞子句，表示「即使當……」。
evolution 演化elegance 優雅survival 生存

文法解析

Grammar Focus

一、so ... that 結果子句

【引句 Quote】
Some creatures possess traits so unusual that they seem like pure fiction.

【句型】
S + V + O + so + adj. + that + S + V ...

【核心說明】
so...that 用來表示「如此……以至於……」。這個句型能把特徵的程度和造成的結果連在一起，讓描述更有力量。

【使用規則】

  • so 後面接形容詞或副詞。
  • that 後面接完整子句。
  • 若後面是名詞片語，常改用 such...that。
  • that 在口語中有時省略，但正式寫作建議保留。

【主題例句】
The frog's defense is so strange that it sounds impossible.
（這種青蛙的防衛方式奇怪到聽起來不可能。）

【學習提醒】
不要把 so 和 very 混用。very 只加強程度；so...that 會帶出結果。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ The trait is so unusual to it seems fictional.
✓ The trait is so unusual that it seems fictional.（結果子句要用 that 連接。）

  • 選擇：The teeth are ___ tiny that they are hard to see. (A) so (B) such (C) very
    答案：A

二、分詞片語表結果

【引句 Quote】
This structure works like a cheese grater, allowing them to scrape and grind food with surprising efficiency.

【句型】
S + V ..., V-ing ...

【核心說明】
句尾 V-ing 分詞片語常用來補充前面整件事造成的自然結果。這裡 allowing... 說明「齒舌像刨絲器運作」帶來的效果：蝸牛能刮取並磨碎食物。

【使用規則】

  • 分詞片語可補充原因、結果或附帶狀況。
  • 句尾分詞片語前通常加逗號。
  • 分詞片語的邏輯主詞通常是前面整件事。
  • to scrape and grind 中 to 是不定詞符號，不是介係詞。

【主題例句】
The red fluid protects the hippo's skin, keeping wounds clean.
（紅色液體保護河馬皮膚，讓傷口保持乾淨。）

【學習提醒】
若要表目的，用 to V；若要補充自然結果，常用 V-ing。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ The radula scrapes food, to making eating efficient.
✓ The radula scrapes food, making eating efficient.（表結果用 V-ing。）

  • 改錯：The frog breaks its bones, to forming claws.
    答案：to forming 改為 forming 或 to form，依語意分別表示結果或目的。

三、When + 過去分詞省略句

【引句 Quote】
When threatened, this creature deliberately fractures its own toe bones...

【句型】
When + Vpp, S + V ...

【核心說明】
When threatened 是省略句，完整形式是 when it is threatened。當副詞子句的主詞和主要子句主詞相同，且含有 be 動詞時，常可省略主詞與 be 動詞。

【使用規則】

  • 省略前後主詞必須相同。
  • 被動狀態常用 Vpp，如 threatened、injured、recognized。
  • 主動動作則常用 V-ing。
  • 省略句讓文章更簡潔，但不可造成主詞不清。

【主題例句】
When attacked, the animal releases a bitter fluid.
（受到攻擊時，這種動物會釋放苦味液體。）

【學習提醒】
先還原完整句子確認主詞。若還原後主詞不同，就不適合省略。

【⚠️ 常見錯誤 / 🎯 精選試題】
✗ When threatened, sharp claws are formed by the frog deliberately.
✓ When threatened, the frog deliberately forms sharp claws.（主要子句主詞應明確指向被威脅的 frog。）

  • 選擇：When ___, hippos produce a protective fluid. (A) exposing to heat (B) exposed to heat (C) expose to heat
    答案：B

練文筆食材⌄

① be full of + N（充滿……）
→ 適用：介紹主題特色。
例：The animal kingdom is full of surprises.

② From A to B（從 A 到 B）
→ 適用：列出範圍。
例：From tiny insects to giant whales, nature is full of wonders.

③ so + adj. + that + S + V（如此……以至於……）
→ 適用：強調程度與結果。
例：The trait is so strange that it seems fictional.

④ called + N（被稱為……）
→ 適用：介紹專有名稱。
例：Snails have a tongue called a radula.

⑤ covered with + N（覆蓋著……）
→ 適用：描述身體構造。
例：The tongue is covered with tiny teeth.

⑥ allowing + O + to V（使……能夠……）
→ 適用：補充結果或功能。
例：The structure works like a grater, allowing snails to grind food.

⑦ Far from being + adj./N（絕非……）
→ 適用：推翻常見印象。
例：Far from being weak, the animal is well-equipped.

⑧ Consequently, S + V（因此，……）
→ 適用：呈現因果關係。
例：Consequently, the squid must eat carefully.

⑨ such as + N（例如……）
→ 適用：補充例子。
例：Large fragments, such as crab shells, can be dangerous.

⑩ force + O + to V（迫使……做……）
→ 適用：描述限制與結果；to 是不定詞。
例：This arrangement forces squid to break down food.

⑪ When + Vpp, S + V（當被……時，……）
→ 適用：簡潔描述被動狀態。
例：When threatened, the frog forms sharp claws.

⑫ recover from + N（從……恢復）
→ 適用：描述復原。
例：Scientists do not know how these frogs recover from injuries.

⑬ function as + N（作為……發揮功能）
→ 適用：說明用途。
例：The fluid functions as sunscreen.

⑭ Taken together, S + V（整體而言，……）
→ 適用：總結多個例子。
例：Taken together, these facts show nature's creativity.

⑮ even when + S + V（即使……）
→ 適用：表示讓步。
例：Evolution aims for survival, even when the result looks weird.
