Day 1　全新的冒險A Brand-New Adventure

第一部分　課文

Last week, Leo finished junior high school. At first, he felt a little lost about the long summer. Then he made a simple plan. First, he wanted to improve his English. After that, he hoped to make new friends. Finally, he decided to join a summer English camp. That night, he packed his bag and smiled. A brand-new adventure was about to begin. 63 words

第二部分　單字介紹

1. improve [ɪmˈpruv] v. 改善、進步
衍生詞　improvement n. 改善、進步

① I want to improve my English this summer.
我想在這個暑假改善英文。

② She has made great improvement in math.
她的數學進步很多。

2. decide [dɪˈsaɪd] v. 決定
衍生詞　decision n. 決定

① He decided to join the camp.
他決定參加營隊。

② It was a hard decision to make.
那是個難下的決定。

3. adventure [ədˈvɛntʃɚ] n. 冒險
衍生詞　adventurous adj. 愛冒險的

① A new adventure was about to begin.
一場新冒險即將展開。

② My brother is adventurous and loves hiking.
我哥哥愛冒險，喜歡健行。

4. simple [ˈsɪmpl̩] adj. 簡單的
衍生詞　simply adv. 簡單地、僅僅

① Leo made a simple plan for his summer.
里歐為暑假做了個簡單的計畫。

② I simply forgot to call you.
我只是忘了打電話給你。

5. finally [ˈfaɪnl̩ɪ] adv. 最後、終於
衍生詞　final adj. 最後的；n. 期末考 / 決賽

① Finally, he decided to join a camp.
最後，他決定參加營隊。

② We will take the final exam next week.
我們下週考期末考。

第三部分　文法講解

文法一：簡單過去式（Simple Past Tense）
表示「過去已經完成的動作」。規則動詞加 -ed（finish → finished、want → wanted、decide → decided）；不規則動詞需個別記憶（make → made、feel → felt）。
課文例：He made a simple plan. ／ He decided to join a camp.
文法二：順序副詞（Sequence Adverbs）
用來把事件依先後順序串起來：first（首先）→ then（接著）→ after that（之後）→ finally（最後）。多放句首，後面常加逗號。
課文例：First, he wanted to improve his English. Finally, he decided to join a camp.

第四部分　練習

A. 單字選擇
1. Leo wanted to ＿＿＿ his English during the summer.
(A) improve　(B) improvement　(C) simple　(D) finally
2. After a lot of thinking, he finally made a big ＿＿＿.
(A) decide　(B) decision　(C) decided　(D) adventure
B. 填充翻譯（每題 3 個空格）
1. 最後，他決定參加一個暑期英語營。
＿＿＿, he ＿＿＿ to ＿＿＿ a summer English camp.
2. 我想在這個夏天改善英文並交新朋友。

Day 2　第一週My First Week

Leo has never felt so excited about studying English. He has practiced speaking every single day. So far, this summer has truly been wonderful. He cannot wait for tomorrow. 64 words

3. excited [ɪkˈsaɪtɪd] adj. 興奮的
衍生詞　excitement n. 興奮、刺激

里歐從沒這麼興奮過。

② The camp was full of excitement.
營隊充滿刺激與興奮。

4. kind [kaɪnd] adj. 親切的
衍生詞　kindness n. 善意、親切

① The teachers have been kind.

② I will never forget your kindness.

① This summer has been wonderful.

② I wonder where he is now.
我想知道他現在在哪裡。

第三部分　文法講解

文法一：現在完成式（Present Perfect Tense）
結構為「have / has + 過去分詞」，表示「過去發生、延續到現在」或「到目前為止的經驗」。第三人稱單數用 has。
課文例：He has learned many new things. ／ Leo has never felt so excited.
文法二：for 與 since
兩者都搭配現在完成式。for 後接「一段時間」（for one week）；since 後接「過去的某個時間點」（since the first day）。
課文例：Leo has been here for one week. ／ Since the first day, he has learned a lot.

② Each contestant gave a short talk.
每位參賽者做了簡短演說。

3. fluently [ˈfluəntlɪ] adv. 流利地
衍生詞　fluent adj. 流利的

① Mia spoke English fluently.
米雅英文說得流利。

② He is fluent in three languages.
他能流利使用三種語言。

4. confident [ˈkɑnfədənt] adj. 有自信的
衍生詞　confidence n. 自信

① Leo grew more confident.
里歐變得更有自信。

② Speaking in public builds confidence.

5. speaker [ˈspikɚ] n. 演說者
衍生詞　speak v. 說、講

① She was the best speaker in the group.
她是組內最棒的演說者。

② Please speak more slowly.
請說慢一點。

第三部分　文法講解

文法一：比較級（Comparatives）
比較「兩者」。短形容詞加 -er（hard → harder）；長形容詞前加 more（more fun、more confident），後面常接 than。
課文例：more fun than the writing class ／ more confident than before.
文法二：最高級（Superlatives）
比較「三者以上」，表示「最…」。短形容詞加 -est 並冠 the（the best）；長形容詞用 the most（the most exciting）。
課文例：the most exciting of all ／ the best speaker in their group.

(A) confident　(B) confidence　(C) active　(D) fluent

第三部分　文法講解

文法一：條件句 if（第一類條件句）
表示「未來可能發生」的假設。結構為「If + 主詞 + 現在式, 主詞 + will / can + 原形」。
課文例：If we work together, we can still succeed. ／ If you believe in yourself, you will find a way.
文法二：連接詞 because 與 so
because 引導「原因」（because + 原因）；so 引導「結果」（…, so + 結果）。同一句通常只用其中一個。
課文例：Because everyone was nervous, they almost gave up. ／ Mia got sick, so the team was worried.

Day 7　最棒的夏天The Best Summer

1. enjoy [ɪnˈdʒɔɪ] v. 享受、喜歡

他的戲劇深受大家喜愛。

② We had a lovely time together.

3. proud [praʊd] adj. 驕傲的
衍生詞　pride n. 驕傲、自豪

里歐為他的隊伍感到驕傲。

② She looked at the prize with pride.

4. hope [hop] v./n. 希望
衍生詞　hopeful adj. 充滿希望的

他希望明年再參加營隊。

② They felt hopeful about the future.

5. life [laɪf] n. 生活、人生
衍生詞　lively adj. 有活力的

① It was the best time of his life.
那是他人生中最棒的時光。

② The classroom was full of lively students.
教室裡滿是充滿活力的學生。

第三部分　文法講解

文法一：被動語態（Passive Voice）
強調「動作的接受者」。結構為「be 動詞 + 過去分詞」，動作者可用 by 帶出。
課文例：The show was watched by many parents. ／ Prizes were given to the best teams.
文法二：動名詞 vs 不定詞（V-ing / to V）
有些動詞後接動名詞（enjoy + V-ing），有些後接不定詞（hope + to V）；動名詞也可當主詞。
課文例：Leo enjoyed performing. ／ He hoped to join the camp. ／ Learning English became his hobby.

2. Leo's parents looked at him with ＿＿＿.
(A) proud　(B) pride　(C) lovely　(D) hopeful
