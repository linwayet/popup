🔍 錯誤深度解析

Q23：關係代名詞受格 whom，學生選 C (who)

句型：...400,000 Jews were forced into the area, many of whom died from disease.
介系詞 of 後面必須接受格，先行詞是人（Jews），所以用 whom（受格）而非 who（主格）。
口訣：介系詞 + whom；動詞主語位置 + who。

Q25：與過去相反假設語氣，學生選 B (hasn't saved)，正確是 C (hadn't saved)

結構：If she hadn't saved the children, they would have ended up in a concentration camp.
與過去事實相反的假設：If + 主詞 + had(n't) + p.p.，主句 would/could + have + p.p.
學生選的 hasn't saved 是現在完成式（直述語氣），用在假設語氣中是常見錯誤。

Q26：片語 To this day，學生選 C (Now and then)

To this day = 直到今天（仍然如此），強調持續至今。
Now and then = 偶爾、不時。
題意：「她直到今日仍被視為英雄」，需要表達「延續至今」的意思，應選 To this day。

Q31：maintenance（維護），學生選 D (intention)

句意：「缺乏適當的維護可能降低樹木的長期生態效益」。
maintenance = 維護、保養（名詞）。intention = 意圖、目的，語意完全不同。
考點：maintenance 搭配 lack of proper maintenance（缺乏適當維護）是常見慣用搭配。

Q34：關係副詞 where，學生選 B (which)

句型：He rides to the train station where he and other dabbawalas sort the lunch boxes.
先行詞是地點（station），後面子句結構完整（有主詞 he、動詞 sort），所以用關係副詞 where，不能用 which（which 後需接不完整子句）。

Q35：have difficulty + V-ing，學生選 A (to find)，正確是 B (finding)

固定句型：have difficulty/trouble (in) + V-ing（做某事有困難）
例：dabbawalas have no difficulty finding their rightful owners.
不能說 have difficulty to find ✗。類似句型：have fun V-ing / spend time V-ing。

whom（關代受格） 假設語氣過去完成 where（關係副詞） have difficulty V-ing To this day maintenance

Q53：慣用語 It stuck，學生選 B（全新創作）

Stick（黏、留存）在此作慣用語：某個名字「就這樣被留用下來了、沿用下去」。
正確答案 (C) It was so impressive（令人印象深刻，因此被記住沿用）是對 It stuck 語境最接近的詮釋。
(B) brand-new creation 是「全新創作」，與語意相反——這個名字不是被設計出來的，是偶然喊出後「留下來的」。

Q54：結尾段功能，學生選 D（總結主旨）

最後段說 Fanta 戰後拓展口味、走向全球，提供的是 Fanta 的後續發展事實，而非總結全文主旨，對應 (C) By providing further facts。
(D) Summarizing 指「重新整理之前說過的重點」，最後段並沒有這樣做，而是補充了新資訊。

🥤 Fanta 文章精讀（重點標注版）

🟡 黃底 = 考題關鍵句  🔵 藍底粗體 = 重要詞彙

When it comes to orange soda, what pops out in your mind? Fanta will probably be the answer. However, this colorful and fruity drink usually associated with energy and youngsters today actually has a humble start.


In the winter of 1940, a whole year after WWII broke out, Max Keith, head of Coca-Cola's German subsidiary, sat in his small office and stared at an empty row of syrup barrels. The war had cut off the familiar shipments of ingredients. The sign outside still read Coca-Cola, but inside the plant the machines hissed and the shelves were bare. ← Q52：此段目的是描述 Keith 的絕境（desperate situation）


Keith refused to shut the doors. He gathered his chemist team and gave them a single, strange instruction: use your imagination. With scarce supplies, imagination would have to be the new raw material. They scoured what was left in the region but only found apple pomace from pressing, whey from dairy farms, and beet leftovers — odd components no one would have thought to blend into a soda. ← Q51：原料 = 農業廢料副產品 (B)
Mixed and tested dozens of times, their "invention" tasted rough and curious at first but then turned to be unexpectedly bright. One afternoon, someone in the team screamed aloud: Fantasie — imagination in German. It stuck; shortened for the label, it became Fanta. ← Q53：It stuck = 這名字被留用下來，令人印象深刻 (C)


At first Fanta rolled out quietly in wartime Germany where Coca-Cola could no longer be made. It wasn't born of market research but of shortages and stubbornness — a drink invented to keep a factory running and people smiling in hard times. When the war ended, Coca-Cola returned to rebuild its global business. The little soda called Fanta stayed with the company. Since then, it has taken on new life with a range of fruit flavors developed later and expanded its playful imagination internationally as its name suggests. ← Q54：最後段補充戰後發展的進一步事實 (C)

🧩 篇章結構解題策略練習

解題三步驟

  • 看空格前一句的尾巴：它談什麼主題？後面的句子應延伸或補充。
  • 看空格後一句的開頭：it / they / these 指代什麼？可以反推空格內容。
  • 排除法：確認選項是否有時間或邏輯上的衝突。

📐 四大必修文法點

① 假設語氣：與過去事實相反（Q25）

If + 主詞 + had (not) + p.p. , 主詞 + would/could + have + p.p.
  • If she hadn't saved the children, they would have ended up in a camp. ✓
  • If she didn't save... ✗（現在假設語氣，時態不對）
  • If she hasn't saved... ✗（現在完成式直述語氣，不是假設語氣）

② 關係代名詞 who vs. whom（Q23）

介系詞 + whom（受格）｜ 主格位置 → who
  • Many of whom died... （of 後 = 受格 = whom）✓
  • The woman who helped them... （主格）✓
  • The woman whom I met... （受格：I met her）✓

③ 關係副詞 where（Q34）

地點先行詞 + where + 完整子句（有主詞＋動詞）
  • He rides to the station where he sorts the boxes. ✓
  • He rides to the station which he sorts the boxes. ✗（which 後子句不完整，缺受詞）
  • 比較：the station which he visited（visited 後缺受詞 → 用 which）✓

④ have difficulty / trouble + V-ing（Q35）

have difficulty / trouble / fun / a good time + (in) + V-ing
  • They have no difficulty finding their rightful owners. ✓
  • I had trouble sleeping last night. ✓
  • have difficulty to find ✗（不可接不定詞）
